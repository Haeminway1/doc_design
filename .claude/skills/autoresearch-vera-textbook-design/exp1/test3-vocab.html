DAY 15

경제/금융 어휘

Economy & Finance — 편입 핵심 출제 어휘 10선

Vera's Tip
오늘의 단어들은 경제·금융 지문에서 반복 출제된다. 동의어를 묶어서 암기하면 문맥 추론 문제에서 속도가 2배 빨라진다.
01 austerity [ɔːstérəti] n.

긴축, 내핍; 검소함

The government introduced austerity measures to reduce the deficit.

Syn. frugality, stringency, restraint

02 recession [riséʃən] n.

경기 침체, 불황

The country fell into a deep recession after the banking crisis.

Syn. downturn, slump, depression

03 liquidity [likwídəti] n.

유동성; 현금화 가능성

Banks must maintain adequate liquidity to meet withdrawal demands.

Syn. solvency, fluidity, availability

04 inflation [infléiʃən] n.

인플레이션, 물가 상승

Rising inflation eroded the purchasing power of consumers.

Syn. price rise, cost increase, escalation

05 collateral [kəlǽtərəl] n./adj.

담보(물); 부수적인

He used his house as collateral for the business loan.

Syn. security, guarantee, pledge

06 depreciate [dipríːʃièit] v.

가치가 하락하다; 평가절하하다

The local currency depreciated sharply against the dollar.

Syn. devalue, decline, diminish

07 fiscal [fískəl] adj.

재정(상)의, 회계의

The new fiscal policy aimed to stimulate economic growth.

Syn. financial, budgetary, monetary

08 dividend [dívədend] n.

배당금; 이익 배분

The company paid a generous dividend to its shareholders.

Syn. profit share, return, yield

09 speculate [spékjulèit] v.

투기하다; 추측하다

Investors began to speculate on emerging market assets.

Syn. gamble, conjecture, theorize

10 insolvency [insɔ́lvənsi] n.

지급 불능, 파산 상태

The firm filed for protection after declaring insolvency.

Syn. bankruptcy, default, failure
