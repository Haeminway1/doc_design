Word Entries — 10 Core Vocabulary
01 deficit [défisit] n.

적자, 부족액

The government ran a fiscal deficit of over $200 billion last year.

syn.shortfall, deficiency, imbalance

02 liquidity [likwiditi] n.

유동성, 현금화 가능성

Banks must maintain adequate liquidity to meet withdrawal demands.

syn.solvency, fluidity, cash flow

03 depreciation [dipri:ʃiéiʃən] n.

감가상각, (통화) 절하

Annual depreciation of machinery reduces the company's taxable income.

syn.devaluation, wear, amortization

04 austerity [ɔːstériti] n.

긴축 (재정), 내핍

The IMF urged the nation to adopt strict austerity measures to stabilize its economy.

syn.austereness, frugality, stringency

05 leverage [lévaridʒ] n. / v.

레버리지, 부채 활용; 영향력을 활용하다

Investors use leverage to amplify potential returns, but it also magnifies risk.

syn.influence, clout, financial gearing

06 collateral [kəlǽtərəl] n. / adj.

담보(물); 부수적인

The bank required real estate as collateral before approving the loan.

syn.security, guarantee, pledge

07 inflation [infléiʃən] n.

인플레이션, 물가 상승

Persistent inflation erodes purchasing power and destabilizes household budgets.

syn.price surge, cost-push, stagflation

08 hedge [hedʒ] v. / n.

위험을 분산하다; 헤지(위험 회피 수단)

Companies hedge against currency fluctuations by using forward contracts.

syn.offset, safeguard, counterbalance

09 solvency [sɑ́lvənsi] n.

지급 능력, 재무 건전성

Regulators monitor solvency ratios to ensure financial institutions remain stable.

syn.financial health, creditworthiness, viability

10 arbitrage [ɑ́:rbitrɑ:ʒ] n.

차익 거래, 재정 거래

Traders exploit arbitrage opportunities when the same asset is priced differently across markets.

syn.price-gap trading, spread trading, riskless profit
