구(Phrase)와 절(Clause)의 근본적 차이

구 vs 절: 핵심 구분법

구 (Phrase)절 (Clause)
정의주어+동사 없이 의미 단위를 이루는 어구주어+동사를 포함하는 의미 단위
예시sitting on the bench (분사구)who is sitting on the bench (관계사절)
독립성문장 성분으로만 기능독립절은 혼자 문장 가능

편입에서 묻는 포인트

  1. 밑줄 친 부분의 문법적 기능 → 구인지 절인지 판별
  2. 구 ↔ 절 전환 → 같은 의미를 구로/절로 바꾸기
  3. 접속사/관계사 선택 → 절을 이끄는 적절한 연결어

빠른 판별법

밑줄 친 부분에 주어+동사가 보이면 절, 보이지 않으면 구!

  • to부정사구: to + 동사원형 (주어 없음) → 구
  • 분사구문: V-ing / p.p. (주어 없음) → 구
  • 전치사구: 전치사 + 명사 → 구
  • 관계사절: 관계사 + S + V → 절
  • 부사절: 접속사 + S + V → 절

1. 관계사절과 관계대명사 선택

핵심 개념: 관계대명사의 종류와 선택 기준

관계대명사는 선행사의 종류와 절 안에서의 역할에 따라 선택한다.

관계대명사선행사역할예문
who사람주어The man who called you is my boss.
whom사람목적어The girl whom I met was kind.
which사물/동물주어/목적어The car which I bought is red.
that사람/사물주어/목적어The movie that we watched was great.
whose사람/사물소유격The boy whose father is a doctor…
when시간부사The day when I met her was special.
where장소부사The city where I was born is small.

판별법: 3단계 선택 전략

  1. 선행사 확인 → 사람? 사물? 시간? 장소?
  2. 관계사절 내 빈자리 확인 → 주어? 목적어? 소유격? 부사?
  3. 제한적 vs 계속적 용법 → 콤마(,) 유무 확인. 콤마 뒤에는 that 불가!

주의: that을 쓸 수 없는 경우

  • 콤마(,) 뒤의 계속적 용법: My car, which is red, … (that 불가)
  • 전치사 바로 뒤: the house in which I live (in that 불가)
The man who called you is my boss. → 선행사: man(사람), 역할: 주어 → who The car which I bought is red. → 선행사: car(사물), 역할: 목적어 → which/that The boy whose father is a doctor studies hard. → 소유격 → whose The day when I met her was special. → 선행사: day(시간), 역할: 부사 → when

예문 연습

1. I know the girl who won the contest. (나는 그 대회에서 우승한 소녀를 안다.) 2. This is the book which changed my life. (이것이 내 인생을 바꾼 책이다.) 3. The house whose roof is red belongs to my uncle. (지붕이 빨간 집은 삼촌 것이다.) 4. Do you remember the place where we first met? (우리가 처음 만난 장소를 기억하니?)

자주 헷갈리는 포인트

  • who vs whom: 절 안에서 주어면 who, 목적어면 whom. 헷갈리면 he/him으로 대입 → he가 맞으면 who, him이 맞으면 whom
  • which vs that: 대부분 호환 가능하지만, 콤마 뒤(계속적 용법)에서는 which만 가능
  • that vs what: that은 선행사가 있고, what은 선행사를 포함(= the thing which)
  • where vs which: 장소 선행사여도 절 안에 전치사가 없으면 which/that (The city that I visited → 목적어 빈자리)

2. 분사구문 ↔ 관계사절 전환

핵심 개념: 분사구(구) vs 관계사절(절)

분사구문은 관계사절에서 관계대명사 + be동사를 생략한 축약형이다.

분사구문 (구)관계사절 (절)
The boy sitting on the benchThe boy who is sitting on the bench
The letter written in EnglishThe letter which was written in English
Department, long reputed to be…Department, which has long been reputed to be…

전환 공식

  1. 능동 (현재분사 -ing): 관계대명사 + be + V-ing → V-ing만 남김
    who is sitting → sitting
  2. 수동 (과거분사 p.p.): 관계대명사 + be + p.p. → p.p.만 남김
    which was written → written
  3. 완료 분사: 관계대명사 + have + p.p. → having p.p.
    who has finished → having finished

편입 빈출 포인트

분사의 능동/수동 판별이 핵심! 수식받는 명사와의 관계를 확인:

  • 명사가 동작을 하면 → 현재분사(-ing): a sleeping baby
  • 명사가 동작을 당하면 → 과거분사(p.p.): a broken window
The girl standing by the door = The girl who is standing by the door (능동 → -ing) The report submitted yesterday = The report which was submitted yesterday (수동 → p.p.) Anyone wanting to join should apply. = Anyone who wants to join should apply. The bridge built in 1990 is now closed. = The bridge which was built in 1990 is now closed.

예문 연습

1. 문 옆에 서 있는 소녀 → The girl standing by the door 2. 어제 제출된 보고서 → The report submitted yesterday 3. 영어로 쓰인 편지 → The letter written in English 4. 벤치에 앉아 있는 소년 → The boy sitting on the bench

자주 헷갈리는 포인트

  • 현재분사(-ing) vs 과거분사(p.p.): 능동/수동 판별이 핵심. "exciting news(흥분시키는)" vs "excited people(흥분된)"
  • 분사구문의 위치: 명사 바로 뒤 → 형용사적 후치수식 / 문장 앞·뒤 → 부사적 분사구문
  • 관계대명사+be 생략 조건: be동사가 있어야 생략 가능. The man who likes coffee → liking 단순 전환 불가
  • 완료 분사 vs 단순 분사: 시제 차이가 있으면 having p.p. 사용

3. 부사 역할을 하는 구와 절

핵심 개념: 부사 역할의 구 vs 절

부사는 동사, 형용사, 다른 부사, 문장 전체를 수식한다. '구'는 주어+동사가 없고, '절'은 주어+동사가 있다.

부사구 (주어+동사 없음)부사절 (주어+동사 있음)
to부정사구: To pass the exam, he studied hard.시간절: When he arrived, I was sleeping.
전치사구: Despite the rain, we went out.조건절: If it rains, we will stay home.
분사구문: Walking home, I saw a cat.양보절: Although he tried, he failed.
이유절: Because she was tired, she left early.
목적절: He saved money so that he could travel.
결과절: It was so cold that the lake froze.

부사절 접속사 핵심 정리

기능접속사
시간when, while, before, after, until, since, as soon as, once
조건if, unless, provided (that), as long as, in case
양보although, though, even though, even if, while
이유because, since, as, now that
목적so that, in order that, lest
결과so … that, such … that

편입 빈출: 부사절 → 분사구문 전환

접속사 + 주어 + 동사 → (접속사 생략) + 분사
Because he was tired, he left. → Being tired, he left. → Tired, he left.

To improve his English, he watched American dramas. (to부정사구 — 목적) Despite the heavy traffic, she arrived on time. (전치사구 — 양보) Walking along the river, I found a beautiful park. (분사구문 — 동시동작) Although it was expensive, she bought the bag. (부사절 — 양보)

예문 연습

1. 시험에 합격하기 위해 그는 열심히 공부했다. → To pass the exam, he studied hard. 2. 비가 옴에도 불구하고 우리는 외출했다. → Despite the rain, we went out. 3. 그가 도착했을 때 나는 자고 있었다. → When he arrived, I was sleeping. 4. 너무 피곤해서 그녀는 일찍 떠났다. → Because she was tired, she left early.

자주 헷갈리는 포인트

  • because vs because of: because + 절(S+V), because of + 명사(구)
  • although vs despite: although + 절, despite + 명사(구)
  • so that(목적) vs so…that(결과): He studied hard so that he could pass.(목적) / He was so tired that he fell asleep.(결과)
  • if vs unless: unless = if … not
  • while(시간) vs while(양보): 문맥으로 판단. "While I was eating(시간)" vs "While I agree with you(양보)"
연습 3 부사 역할을 하는 구와 절 연습문제 (20문제)

1. ______ it was raining heavily, we decided to cancel the picnic.

① Despite   ② Although   ③ Because   ④ Because of   

2. ______ the bad weather, the event was held outdoors.

① Although   ② Despite   ③ Because   ④ Since   

3. She studied abroad ______ she could improve her language skills.

① so that   ② because   ③ although   ④ unless   

4. ______ you finish your homework, you cannot go out.

① If   ② Unless   ③ Although   ④ Because   

5. He was ______ tired ______ he fell asleep during the meeting.

① so / that   ② such / that   ③ too / to   ④ so / as   

6. ______ arriving at the station, she realized she had left her ticket at home.

① Until   ② Upon   ③ During   ④ While   

7. ______ he apologized, she refused to forgive him.

① Because   ② Even though   ③ So that   ④ Unless   

8. I'll wait here ______ you come back.

① until   ② during   ③ while   ④ by   

9. ______ his lack of experience, he performed remarkably well.

① Although   ② In spite of   ③ Because of   ④ Since   

10. ______ the sun sets, the temperature drops rapidly.

① Once   ② Despite   ③ Unless   ④ In case   

11. Take an umbrella ______ it rains.

① in case   ② unless   ③ although   ④ so that   

12. She left early ______ avoid the rush-hour traffic.

① so that   ② in order to   ③ because   ④ although   

13. ______ working on the project, he discovered a major error.

① While   ② During   ③ Although   ④ Despite   

14. ______ she is young, she has a lot of experience.

① Because   ② Although   ③ Unless   ④ Until   

15. He spoke slowly ______ everyone could understand him.

① so that   ② because   ③ although   ④ even if   

16. ______ I was having dinner, the phone rang.

① During   ② While   ③ Despite   ④ Because of   

17. It was ______ a difficult exam ______ most students failed.

① so / that   ② such / that   ③ too / to   ④ very / that   

18. ______ you study hard, you will not pass the exam.

① If   ② Unless   ③ Although   ④ When   

19. He saved money ______ he could buy a new laptop.

① in order that   ② because   ③ although   ④ while   

20. ______ rich he may be, he cannot buy happiness.

① Although   ② However   ③ Despite   ④ Even   

연습 3. 부사 역할을 하는 구와 절 정답 및 해설

#정답해설
1③뒤에 절(it was raining) + 이유 → Because
2②뒤에 명사구(the bad weather) + 양보 → Despite
3①목적(~하기 위해) → so that + could
4②숙제를 끝내지 않으면(= if you don't finish) → Unless
5①so + 형용사(tired) + that + 절 → 결과 구문
6②Upon + V-ing = ~하자마자(= As soon as she arrived)
7②사과했음에도 용서 안 함 → 양보 Even though
8①네가 돌아올 때까지 → until + 절
9②뒤에 명사구(his lack) + 양보 → In spite of (= Despite)
10①해가 지면(일단 ~하면) → Once + 절
11①비가 올 경우를 대비해서 → in case
12②to부정사구(목적): ~하기 위해 → in order to + 동사원형
13①While + V-ing(분사구문) = ~하는 동안. During은 명사 앞에만 사용
14②젊음에도 경험 많음 → 양보 Although
15①모두가 이해할 수 있도록(목적) → so that
16②뒤에 절(I was having) + 시간(~하는 동안) → While
17②such + a(n) + 형용사 + 명사 + that → 결과 구문
18②열심히 공부하지 않으면(= If you don't) → Unless
19①새 노트북을 살 수 있도록(목적) → in order that (= so that)
20②However + 형용사 + S + V = 아무리 ~해도 (양보의 부사절)
