**지문 내용:**
Last Tuesday, Sujin was walking home from the library. She was thinking about her homework. [She found a] brown leather wallet on the sidewalk. She picked it up and opened it. Inside, there was some money and a driver's license. Sujin realized that she should find the owner. She felt a little bit of panic, not knowing what to do. Then she saw a police station across the street. She went inside and gave the wallet to a police officer. The next day, the owner called her. He was the owner of the wallet. He was very thankful and called her an honest student. He even sent a small reward to her school. Sujin felt proud of doing the right thing.
