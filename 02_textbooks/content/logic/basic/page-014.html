008.

To say that a believer is happier than a skeptic is no more to the point than to say that a drunken man is happier than a sober one.

"~이 아니다"

He is no more a genius than I am.

A whale is no more a fish than a horse.

She is no more capable of lying than an angel.

재진술 :

  1. As a drunken man is happier than a sober one, so a believer is happier than a skeptic.
  2. It is safe to say that a skeptic is happier than a believer as long as he stays away from alcohol.
  3. It makes sense that alcohol does not make people any happier, nor does religious faith.
  4. To say that a believer is happier is more important than to say that a drunken man is happier.

009.

재진술 :

  1. Comic books were not viewed as important by social critics for a while.
  2. Social critics have been trying to reveal the importance of comic books.
  3. Social critics occasionally recognized the importance of comic books.
  4. Comic books have been studied for their importance by social critics.
