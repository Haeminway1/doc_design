Step 2. 의미의 재구성: 패러프레이징 (Paraphrasing)

008.

Until the day comes when we expel any form of violence against women, we will be hard pressed to say that gender equality exists in our country.

재진술 :

  1. Violence against women, if not eliminated, will continue to makes it difficult to say that men and women are equal in our country.
  2. Only when women refrain from violence, will we be able to say that our country guarantees gender equality.
  3. Unless we eradicate violence against women, our country will be compelled to declare war against gender inequality.
  4. Increasing unjust violence against women will drive us to protest against gender discrimination in our country.

009.

Managers in commerce and industry must increase efficiency to help get us out of the economic slump, and the same applies to public-service managers.

재진술 :

  1. In order to overcome the economic slump, managers in commerce and industry and public service managers need to boost efficiency.
  2. To maintain the current economic state, efficiency should be increased by managers in commerce and industry and public-service managers.
  3. Managers in commerce and industry need to negotiate with public-service managers to assist in improving the economy.
  4. Unlike public-service managers, managers in commerce and industry must do their share for the economy.
