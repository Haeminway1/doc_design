Step 2. 의미의 재구성: 패러프레이징 (Paraphrasing)

006.

The pain of her injured back was beyond description.

재진술 :

  1. She felt so much pain in the back after she got it injured.
  2. The pain she had finally gotten over could not be explained.
  3. The pain she felt after being attacked defied description.
  4. The backache was relieved gradually after she got injured.

1. beyond A A를 넘어서는 description 말

007.

The important thing in science is not so much to obtain new facts as to discover new ways of thinking about them.

재진술 :

  1. It's important in science to get new facts as well as new insights into them.
  2. Science requires new ways of thought more than a collection of new facts.
  3. To be a scientist, you should distinguish facts from mere thoughts about them.
  4. Research on new facts isn't important in science consisting of new approaches.
