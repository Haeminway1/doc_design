Step 2. 의미의 재구성: 패러프레이징 (Paraphrasing)

030.

Thinking about how these normal people suddenly disappeared, we cannot but accept that humans are transient things.

재진술 :

  1. Since even normal people can report signs of disorientation, we should acknowledge that humans are imperfect things.
  2. Now that ordinary people can suddenly disappear, we have to take steps to prevent the people going missing in the future.
  3. Though ordinary people want to live forever, we cannot realize that we are as evanescent as morning dew.
  4. Based on the thought that normal people can die abruptly, we cannot help embracing the ephemeral nature humans have.
