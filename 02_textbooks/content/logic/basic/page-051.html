Renewable energy sources are becoming increasingly important as fossil fuel reserves decline. Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity without producing harmful emissions. Wind turbines harness natural wind power to generate clean energy. These sustainable technologies offer hope for a greener future while reducing our dependence on fossil fuels.

Many students struggle with procrastination during their academic careers. This habit often stems from fear of failure or perfectionism. To overcome procrastination, students can break large tasks into smaller, manageable steps. Additionally, setting specific deadlines and rewarding progress can help maintain motivation.

이 글의 전개 방식은?

  1. 문제 제시 → 원인 분석 → 해결책 제시
  2. 현상 설명 → 비교 분석 → 결론 도출
  3. 일반론 → 구체적 예시 → 반대 의견
