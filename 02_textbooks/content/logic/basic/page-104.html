Step 2. 의미의 재구성: 패러프레이징 (Paraphrasing)

068.

The company attributed a drop in second-quarter earnings from the first quarter to material costs and a slowing of shipments to the United States.

재진술 :

  1. Material costs and slowed shipments caused second-quarter earnings to be less than those of the first quarter, the company said.
  2. Second-quarter earnings would not have been less than first quarter earnings, if material costs and transportation expense had not skyrocketed.
  3. According to the company report, the scarcity of material and transportation caused earnings drop in the second quarter.
  4. The second-quarter earnings decline accounts for a slower shipments to the United States.

1. attribute A to B A를 B의 탓으로 돌리다 A (attributed to B) 목적

069.

What works in Korea will work in other parts of Asia but not necessarily the other way around.

재진술 :

  1. Those who work hard in Korea will work hard in other Asian nations, but this does not guarantee all the workers in Korea.
  2. Studies in Korea as well as Asia will be conducted across Asia but not necessarily so.
  3. That the degrees earned in Korea will be compatible in other Asian countries is not acceptable anymore in Asia.
  4. What is effective in Korea will also apply to the rest of Asia, but the converse is not always true.
