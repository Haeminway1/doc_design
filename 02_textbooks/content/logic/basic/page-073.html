036.

The author seemed taciturn during his television interview, but when I met him in person, he was quite sociable.

  1. loud and talkative
  2. quiet and reserved
  3. anxious and upset
  4. unhappy and distraught

reserve : 예약하다, 남겨두다, 내성적인 reserve 명사 : 비축물, 보호구역, 내성, 자제심

037.

When Sara was sick, her voice was almost inaudible. We couldn't hear what she was trying to say clearly.

  1. very loud, easy to hear
  2. very soft, hard to hear
  3. very strange, uncommon
  4. very shy, introverted
