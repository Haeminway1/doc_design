Step 2. 의미의 재구성: 패러프레이징 (Paraphrasing)

028.

We are demoralizing our children and giving them a darker sense of their future than is warranted.

재진술 :

  1. We are discouraging our children and implanting in their hearts an (unjustifiably) negative view of their future.
  2. We are corrupting our children and driving them into a less bright future than we think it should be.
  3. We are raising our children above the level of morality and blinding them to their enjoyable future in this world.
  4. We are educating our children on the scientific basis and dooming them to an unendurably bleak future.

029.

The marriage of an old seat of learning with a new industry may produce some interesting children.

재진술 :

  1. The marriage between an old academic family and a new entrepreneurial family may sometimes beget eccentric offspring.
  2. The entrance into a new business after being trained for it may guarantee some youths venture success.
  3. Combining an old atmosphere of learning and elements of a new industry may bring some interesting results.
  4. A new industry deep-rooted in learning may teem with all sorts of brainchildren of geniuses in the field.

1. 'marry' + with + N 배우자와 함께 be V.p.p. to O ~와 결혼하다

2. marry A with B → A와 B를 결합하다.
