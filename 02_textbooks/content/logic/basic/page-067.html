Step 2. 의미의 재구성: 패러프레이징 (Paraphrasing)

026.

I know of no great men except those who have rendered great service to the human.

재진술 :

  1. I know that a great man is not the only one who has done much for the human wellbeing.
  2. I wonder if there is any great man who has been of great service to the human community.
  3. All great men I know have not dared to do something good for their fellow beings.
  4. As far as I know great men are those who have made a great contribution to the humanity.

Know : 사실을 알다 Know of : ~에 대해, 정보를 알다

027.

Afghans are angry that the war against the Taliban has not been won, and that foreign troops have the run of the country.

재진술 :

  1. Afghanistan's defeat in the war against the Taliban has made its people angry and triggered the military invasion by outsiders.
  2. To the anger of Afghans, their country has not yet defeated Taliban and is tramped down by the armies of other countries.
  3. Angry Afghans have not won the war against the Taliban, so foreign countries are dispatching their armies to help them.
  4. The war against the Taliban has brought no gain to Afghanistan, enraging its people who depend on the foreign armed forces.
