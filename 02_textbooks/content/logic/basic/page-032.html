Despite its purported naturally, the study will be compelled to produce results favorable to its sponsors.

재진술 :

  1. The results of the study will be unbiased through the people who fund it do not want them to be.

  3. Even though people say it has to be fair, the report will be supported by financial backers.
  4. The research claims to be fair, but it will be forced to say good things about the people who paid for it.
