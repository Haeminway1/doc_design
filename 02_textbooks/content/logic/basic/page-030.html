Step 2. 의미의 재구성: 패러프레이징 (Paraphrasing)

006.

If greed motivates individuals to work for the good things in life, there may be a silver lining in the trait.

재진술 :

  1. If good things rest on the motivation of greed, it follows that generous people can never do good to others.
  2. If it is possible that people do good things out of greed, it means that greed may have a bright side in it.
  3. Individuals eager to commit themselves to the good in life can retain the trait until they reach old age.
  4. Even greed may count as a valuable thing in a struggling world, as many of greedy people are men of charity.

007.

If we compare the spending habits of females and males in our society, a sharp distinction emerges.

재진술 :

  1. Compared with the spending habits of females, those of males are more rational in our society.
  2. Men and women in our society spend money in a clearly different way from each other.
  3. Classifying the spending habits by gender is emerging as a distinct trend in our society.
  4. The similar and different ways of spending money are found between men and women in our society.
