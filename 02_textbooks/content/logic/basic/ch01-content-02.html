02. 문맥 안에서 이해하라 (Understand in the Context)
다음에 주어질 각 예문에서 밑줄 친 단어의 의미를 결정해주는 것이 무엇인가?
• Trees are large plants.
• This facility is the largest power plant in the country.
• He received a prison term for drunk driving.
• Under the terms of their contract, employees must give 3 months' notice if they leave.
• The king delivered a televised speech to the nation on Nov 5.
• They rushed her to hospital where doctors delivered her baby.

"문맥 없는 단어는 껍데기에 불과하다!"

단어는 문맥을 만나야 비로소 의미가 완성된다.

term (끝. 제한. 한계)

  1. 용어
  2. 기간. 기한. 임기
  3. 조건
  4. 말씨
