Step 2. 의미의 재구성: 패러프레이징 (Paraphrasing)

066.

While it may not be possible to link these environmental changes to global warming, they offer a worrying window into the future.

재진술 :

  1. Though impossible to attribute to global warming, these environmental changes show no good prospects of the future.
  2. Despite the possibility that they are not due to global warming, these environmental changes cast a shadow on the future.
  3. Because we cannot associate these environmental changes with global warming, they will leave worries to the future generation.
  4. In so far as it is possible to think of these environmental changes as a result of global warming, they provide a negative view of the future.

067.

No matter what politicians may claim to stand for, (when push comes to shove) they will do what will get them in power.

재진술 :

  1. Politicians are powerful in that irrespective of their ideology, they can make the world different through further effort.
  2. Whatever politicians proclaim, if properly propagated to their constituents, they can come to power.
  3. The initial peaceful presentations of political views always turn into bitter words or personal attacks in the election.
  4. Politicians set forth this view or that in the campaign, but at any time necessary, give top priority to winning the election.
