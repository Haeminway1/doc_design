046.

While the National Gallery of Art may sound inaccessible for kids, they couldn't have a better place to start an art appreciation.

재진술 :

  1. The National Gallery of Art seems popular with kids but they cannot be best exposed to artistic beauty there.
  2. Children, while staying out of the National Gallery of Art, were unable to find any better place for their art education.
  3. The National Gallery of Art, though difficult for kids to enter, would be the best place for them to begin viewing art works.
  4. There would be no children who can appreciate the collections of the National Gallery of Art not yet open to them.

047.

Every old man (who dies of one cause) has generally several causes present (which would kill him if that one did not.)

재진술 :

  1. Old people generally accept their mortality because if they did not die of one cause, they would die of another.
  2. For most of the elderly people, it is difficult to find out which of some potential causes actually leads to their death.
  3. Generally speaking, each of the elderly dies of the interaction of some causes, each of which has the killing potential.
  4. Every old man in general dies of one of some causes in his body which have the potential to put him to death.
