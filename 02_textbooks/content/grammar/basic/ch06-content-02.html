6-3. 상관 접속사

상관 접속사의 ① 뜻과 짝, ② 병치, 그리고 ③ 수 일치에 유의한다.

짝해석수일치
both A and BA와 B 둘 다복수 취급
either A or BA와 B 중 아무거나 하나동사는 B에 일치
neither A nor BA도 아니고 B도 아닌동사는 B에 일치
not A but BA가 아니라 B동사는 B에 일치
not only A but also BA뿐만 아니라 B도동사는 B에 일치
A as well as BB뿐만 아니라 A도동사는 A에 일치
A (rather than) BB라기 보다는 A동사는 A에 일치
예시: The course consists of not only reading but writing.
그 수업은 읽기뿐만 아니라 쓰기로도 구성되어 있다.
예시: Jane rather than your friends is responsible for the accidents.
네 친구들이라기보다는 Jane이 이번 사고에 책임이 있다.
  • not only A but also B
    = not just A
    = not merely A
    = not simply A
    = B as well as A
6-4. 명사절 접속사

명사절의 종류와 기본 개념

No.종류형태해석의미용법
1 명사절 접속사 that [that 완]=N ~것 ~라는, 기정의 사실 전치사의 목적어만 불가
2 관계 대명사 what [what 불완전]=N ~것 모두가능
3 명사절 접속사 if S+V ~인지 아닌지 모르는, 미정의 내용 진주어, 전목 불가능
4 명사절 접속사 whether S+V ~인지 아닌지 모두 가능 (일반 주어, 전목)
5 간접 의문문 when, where, why, how + S+V 언제, 어디서, 왜, 어떻게 모두가능

다음 문장 속의 [명사절]을 찾아 체크하고 어떤 용법으로 쓰였는지 구분한 후 옳고 그름을 판단하시오.

1. [That he is a genius] is unbelievable. (일반주어)
2. It is unbelievable [that he is a genius]. (진주어)
3. The fact is [that he said so]. (주격보어)
4. I think it a pity [that he has no supporters]. (진목적어)
5. I believe [what we'll be able to help them]. (타동사의 목적어)
6. Doc told me [my ankle wasn't broken] and [that it was just a sprain]. (명사절)
7. He begged forgiveness for [that he had done]. (전치사의 목적어)
8. The budget is unrealistic [in that it disregards increased cost]. (전목적어 불가)
9. I am quite well except [that I have a slight cold]. (전목적어 불가)
10. The rumor [that he died] is not certain. (동격that)
11. I was in charge of a group [that was responsible for all the marketing campaigns of servers]. (관계대명사)
12. He made a false confession [that he later retracted]. (관계 that)
13. The husband's unfaithfulness was [what brought on the dissolution of the marriage]. (주격보어)
14. You're all dying to know [that these cookies taste like]. (타동사의 목적어)
15. [What you are saying] is totally out of the blue and I'm at a loss [what to do]. (일반주어 / 타동사의 목적어)
16. A man's worth lies not so much in [what he has] as in [what he is]. (전치사의 목적어)
17. There is no clear dividing line between [what is good] and [what is bad]. (전목적어)
18. [Whether she likes him] is not clear to me. (일반주어)
19. It is not clear to me (if she likes him). (진목적어)
20. The point is [whether she will like him or not]. (주격보어)
21. Everything depends upon (whether we have enough money). (전목적어)
22. He couldn't decide [whether to go or to turn back]. (타동사의 목적어)
23. I don't care [whether or not your car breaks down]. (타동사의 목적어)
24. I don't know [that it will be raining or not]. (X 미정의내용)
25. [How the book will sell] depends on its author. (일반주어)
26. Don't be anxious about [how fast are you learning]. (전목적어 X)
27. Don't be anxious about [how fast are you learning]. (전목적어 X)
28. [Why he is absent] is because he is ill. (일반주어)
  • [how S + V] = N 어떻게
  • [how 형/부 S + V] = N 얼마나 형/부 한지
