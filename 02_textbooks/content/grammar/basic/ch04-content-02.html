4-3. 구분해야 할 형용사
No.형용사 1뜻 1형용사 2뜻 2예문
1 respectable 존경할 만한 respectful 공손한 He is respectable teacher
You should be respectful to your parents
They went back to their respective hometown. (respective: 각각의)
2 imaginable 상상할 수 있는 imaginary 가상의, 허구의 I have tried every means imaginable.
He wrote an imaginary tale.
He is an imaginative writer. (imaginative: 상상력이 풍부한)
3 economic 경제의 economical 검소한 I am interested in economic policy.
It is economical to buy large quantities.
4 healthful 건강에 좋은 healthy 건강한, 건전한 The food is healthful diet.
He is quite healthy.
5 historic 역사상으로 중요한 historical 역사의 This is a historic building.
I like reading historical novels.
6 industrial 산업의 industrious 근면한, 부지런한 I visited an industrial exhibition.
She proved to be industrious.
7 childlike 천진난만한, 순진한 childish 유치한 I like him because of his childlike behavior.
He is very childish sometimes.
8 considerable 상당한 considerate 사려 깊은, 배려하는 It takes considerable money to buy it.
It was considerate of him to say so.
9 successful 성공적인 successive 연속적인, 계승하는 I am successful in passing the exam.
There were successive disasters.
10 valueless 가치 없는 invaluable 매우 귀중한 These are valueless books.
This is an invaluable book.
11 memorable 기억할 만한 memorial 기념의, 추도의 He delivered a memorable speech.
We prepared a memorial service.
12 practicable 실행/이용 가능한 practical 실용적인 The roads are practicable.
We had a practical test.
13 classic 일류의, 표준적인 classical 고전의, 고전적인 The painting is classic works.
I am ignorant in classical music.
14 literal 문자상의 literary 문학의 Understand the literal meaning of the word.
He won the literary prize.
He has been literate since he was 3. (literate: 글을 읽을 줄 아는)
15 popular 인기 있는, 대중의 populous 인구가 많은 He is a popular professor.
Seoul is a populous city.
16 beneficial 이로운, 유익한 beneficent 자비로운 This is beneficial to health.
He has beneficent parents.
17 luxurious 사치스러운 luxuriant 비옥한, 번성한 This is luxurious hotel.
This crops are of luxuriant growth.
18 momentous 중대한 momentary 순간적인 We made a momentous decision.
A momentary impulse is dangerous thing.
