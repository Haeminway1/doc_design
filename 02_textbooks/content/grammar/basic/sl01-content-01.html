특강 1 비교 [원포인트 편입문법]
SL1-1. 비교의 기본
  • 원급 비교 : as 원급 as (A=B)
  • 차등 비교 : 비교급 than (A>B)
  • 기타 비교 표현들 : be similar to (~와 유사한) / be different (from) (~와 다른)
    be superior to (~보다 우월한) / be inferior to (~보다 열등한)
다음 문장 속의 비교구문을 찾아 옳고 그름을 판단하시오.
She is as smart as her sister.
I cannot walk so quick as you can.
To answer accurately is more important than finishing quickly.
Insurance rates for women are lower than these for men.
The camera looks less expensive than yours.
The current economic crisis is worse than that of the 1990s.
The country is still inferior than the developed countries in the field of engineering.
SL1-2. 배수사 비교

배수사는 비교표현 (as~as, 비교급-than) 앞에 쓴다.

  • 두 배(twice, two times)는 원급비교와 쓴다.
  • 세 배 이상(three times~)은 원급비교, 차등비교에 모두 쓸 수 있다.
(예시)
Taiwan's GNP is nearly twice more than Indonesia's.
He is as three times rich as you.
The amount of salt we eat is about five times much than what we really need.
SL1-3. not so much A as B (A라기보다는 B)
  • = not A so much as B
  • = B (rather than A)
  • = rather B than A
SL1-4. no better than (~과 다름없는)
(예시)
The work environment is no better than modern slavery.
SL1-5. the 비교급 ~, the 비교급 ~ (~할수록 ~해진다)
(예시)
The older you grow, the more wisely you become.
SL1-6. '~는 말할 것도 없고...'
  • 긍정문 + much more, still more, (let alone) not to mention
  • 부정문 + much less, still less, (let alone) not to mention
(예시)
He has no daily necessities, much more luxuries.
His books are easy to understand, still less his lectures.
There isn't enough room for us, let alone any guests.
SL1-7. know better than to V (~할 정도로 어리석지는 않다, ~하는 것 보다는 낫다)
(예시)
He knows better than to believe such a lie.
I am enough wise not to do such a thing.
SL1-8. 최상급 대용 표현
  • = 비교급 than any other 단수명사
  • = 부정주어 + 비교표현
(예시)
Kate is taller than any other girl in her class.
No metal is so precious as gold.
