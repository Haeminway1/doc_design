특강 2 도치 [원포인트 편입문법]
SL2-1. 부정어 도치

문두의 부정어 다음에는 동사 + 주어 순서로 도치된다. (일반동사는 do/does/did 활용)

문두의 부정어 동사 주어 명, 형, 분사 / RV / p.p
① No, Not, Never be
일반 조동사
have, has, had
do, does, did		 S 명, 형, 분사
RV
② hardly, scarcely, rarely, barely, seldom p.p
③ few, little RV
④ only
(예시)
Not a word Jane said to me all day long.
Not only accomplished he great achievements but he was a great scientist.
Never I have seen such a good view.
Hardly have we arrived than the problems started.
Little did they dream that they would win the prize.
Only recently I have heard the news.
Only when you are present he behaves well.
He behaves well only when you are present.
Only after we lose health do we realize the true value of it.
(SL2-1. 부정어 도치 예시)
Not until you told me did I know the fact.
Neither you nor I are to blame for the accident.
Not only you but I am to blame for the accident.
No man was to be seen on the street.
SL2-2. 장소 부사구 도치

문두의 장소표현 다음에는 1형식 자동사 + 주어 순서로 도치된다.

문두의 장소표현 1형식 자동사 주어
(1) Here, There be, exist, stand, lie, remain
come, go, stay, live		 S
(2) 장소를 나타내는 전명구
(예시)
(Here) lie the difficulty.
(On the hills) stand a church.
(Under the car) was some kittens.
(By the fireplace) sits a tallest man in the world.
(To our right) lie the Han River.
(Among the papers) are a proposal to add a choice of optional dental plans to the employee benefits package.
There you go again.
(On the weekends) we go out and have a ball.
