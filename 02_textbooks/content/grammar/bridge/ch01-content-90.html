Ch. 11 편입영어 출제 포인트 및 함정
1. 시간 전치사 혼동

❌ He was born in Christmas Day. → ✅ He was born on Christmas Day.
❌ I'll see you on next Monday. → ✅ I'll see you next Monday. (시간부사 앞 전치사 생략)

2. 장소 전치사 혼동

❌ I'm waiting you in the bus stop. → ✅ I'm waiting for you at the bus stop.
❌ The book is in the table. → ✅ The book is on the table.

3. 동사-전치사 고정조합 오류

❌ I'm looking forward to see you. → ✅ I'm looking forward to seeing you.
❌ Please explain about this problem. → ✅ Please explain this problem. (explain은 타동사)

4. 형용사-전치사 고정조합 오류

❌ I'm interested about music. → ✅ I'm interested in music.
❌ He's married with my sister. → ✅ He's married to my sister.
