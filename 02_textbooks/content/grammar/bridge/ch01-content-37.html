3.4.2. 목적어가 의문사절인 경우

의문사절 전체가 주어가 되거나, 가주어 It을 사용하여 수동태를 만듭니다.

  • 능동: Nobody knows who wrote this letter.
  • 수동 1: Who wrote this letter is not known.
  • 수동 2: It is not known who wrote this letter.
3.4.3. 복합동사(구동사)의 수동태

구동사는 하나의 동사 덩어리로 취급하므로, 전치사나 부사를 절대 빠뜨리면 안 됩니다.

구동사수동태 예문
look afterChildren should be looked after carefully.
take care ofThe patient must be taken care of.
put offThe meeting has been put off.
carry outThe plan will be carried out next week.
laugh atHe doesn't want to be laughed at.
deal withThe problem will be dealt with immediately.
rely onThis data can be relied on.
run overThe dog was almost run over by a car.
bring upThe sensitive issue was brought up during the discussion.
call offThe game had to be called off due to heavy rain.
look down onNo one likes being looked down on for their background.
put up withThis kind of behavior will not be put up with any longer.
