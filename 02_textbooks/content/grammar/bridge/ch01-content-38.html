3.4.3. 복합동사(구동사)의 수동태 (추가 예시)

편입 시험에서는 다양한 구동사의 수동태 활용 능력을 평가합니다. 다음은 시험에 자주 출제되는 추가적인 구동사 목록이므로, 반드시 하나의 덩어리로 인식하고 암기해야 합니다.

구동사수동태 예문
look up toShe is looked up to as a role model by many young artists.
make fun ofThe student was upset after being made fun of by his classmates.
take advantage ofThis special offer should be taken advantage of before it expires.
account forThe missing funds must be accounted for immediately.
do away withThe outdated regulation has finally been done away with.
refer to asThis period in history is referred to as the Renaissance.
think of asHe is thought of as one of the greatest scientists of his generation.
get rid ofThe old files will finally be gotten rid of this weekend.
pay attention toSpecial attention must be paid to the warning labels.
make use ofBetter use should be made of the available resources.
find fault withFault was found with every detail of the proposal.
speak ill ofThe former manager was often spoken ill of by his employees.
take notice ofHis valuable contributions were finally taken notice of by the committee.
set fire toThe abandoned warehouse was set fire to by arsonists.
catch sight ofA rare bird was caught sight of by the birdwatchers.
