Ch. 7 혼동하기 쉬운 수동태 표현들
7.1. 감정 동사의 능동분사(-ing) vs. 수동분사(-ed)

감정 동사는 '~하게 만들다'는 타동사이므로, 감정을 일으키는 주체는 능동분사(-ing), 감정을 느끼는 대상은 수동분사(-ed)를 사용합니다.

-ing (감정 유발)-ed (감정 느낌)예문
interesting (흥미로운)interested (흥미를 느끼는)The book is interesting. / I am interested in the book.
surprising (놀라운)surprised (놀란)The news was surprising. / We were surprised at the news.
boring (지루한)bored (지루해하는)The lecture was boring. / The students were bored.
exciting (신나는)excited (신난)The game is exciting. / The fans are excited.
confusing (혼란스러운)confused (혼란스러워하는)The map is confusing. / I am confused.
tiring (피곤하게 하는)tired (피곤한)The work was tiring. / He is tired from work.
frightening (무서운)frightened (무서워하는)The horror movie was frightening. / She is frightened of spiders.
disappointing (실망스러운)disappointed (실망한)The result was disappointing. / He was disappointed with the result.
satisfying (만족스러운)satisfied (만족한)The meal was very satisfying. / I am satisfied with your work.
amazing (놀라운)amazed (놀란)The view is amazing. / We were amazed by her talent.
