2.3. 관계대명사 `that`의 특별 용법

`that`을 주로 사용하는 경우

  1. 선행사가 `사람 + 사물/동물`일 때:
    e.g., Look at the boy and his dog that are running in the park.
  2. 선행사에 `the only, the very, the same, 서수, 최상급`이 포함될 때:
    e.g., He is the only person that can solve this problem.
    e.g., This is the very book that I was looking for.
  3. 선행사가 `all, every, any, no` 또는 `-thing`으로 끝나는 부정대명사일 때:
    e.g., All that glitters is not gold. / Is there anything that I can do?

`that`을 사용할 수 없는 경우

  1. 계속적 용법 (쉼표 뒤):
    e.g., I met Mr. Kim, who is a famous lawyer. (O) / ..., that is... (X)
  2. 전치사 뒤:
    e.g., This is the house in which I live. (O) / ...in that I live. (X)
