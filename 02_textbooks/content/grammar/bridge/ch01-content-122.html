manage to R가까스로 ~해내다She managed to pass the difficult exam.
needless to say말할 필요도 없이Needless to say, honesty is the best policy.
so to speak말하자면, 비유하자면He is, so to speak, a walking encyclopedia.

to be sure확실히, 틀림없이He is, to be sure, a talented musician, but he lacks discipline.

to tell the truth사실을 말하자면To tell the truth, I forgot about our appointment.
