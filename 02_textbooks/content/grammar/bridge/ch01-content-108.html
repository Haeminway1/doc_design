2.3. 분사구문의 의미별 접속사 변환표

분사구문은 원래 '접속사 + 주어 + 동사' 형태의 부사절을 간결하게 줄인 것입니다. 문맥에 따라 다양한 의미를 가지며, 아래와 같이 원래의 접속사를 복원하여 해석할 수 있습니다.

의미 대표 접속사 분사구문 예시 → 부사절 변환
시간 (Time) when, while, after Walking along the street, I met an old friend.
→ While I was walking...
이유 (Reason) because, as, since Feeling tired, I went to bed early.
→ Because I felt tired...
조건 (Condition) if Turning to the right, you will find the building.
→ If you turn to the right...
양보 (Concession) though, although Admitting what you say, I still think you are wrong.
→ Although I admit what you say...
동시동작 (Simultaneous) while, as She watched TV, eating popcorn.
→ ...while she was eating popcorn.
연속동작 (Consecutive) and He opened the door, finding no one inside.
→ He opened the door and found no one inside.
