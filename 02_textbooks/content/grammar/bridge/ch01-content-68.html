상세 예시로 개념 다지기

등위접속사 (병렬 구조 확인!)

  • The company's goals are to increase profits, to expand its market share, and to improve customer satisfaction.
  • He did not study for the test, so he failed it. (원인, so, 결과)
  • She is a talented musician, yet she suffers from stage fright. (예상 밖의 대조)
  • He can't speak English, nor can he write it. (`nor` 뒤 도치)

상관접속사 (병렬 구조 & 수일치 확인!)

  • Both my brother and I are interested in history. (복수 동사)
  • Neither the manager nor the employees were aware of the new policy. (B에 수일치)
  • The presentation was not only informative but also engaging. (형용사 병렬)
  • The captain, as well as the sailors, was exhausted. (B에 수일치)

전치사 vs. 접속사 비교 (실전 문제 유형)

  • (접속사) He fell asleep while he was watching TV.
  • (전치사) He fell asleep during the movie.
  • (접속사) We canceled the picnic because it started to rain.
  • (전치사) We canceled the picnic because of the rain.
  • (접속사) Although the task was difficult, he managed to complete it.
  • (전치사) Despite the difficulty of the task, he managed to complete it.
