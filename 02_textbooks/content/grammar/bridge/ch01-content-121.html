Ch. 5 주요 부정사 관용 표현

부정사는 다양한 단어와 결합하여 고유한 의미를 갖는 관용 표현을 많이 만듭니다. 이 표현들은 통째로 암기하는 것이 가장 효과적입니다.

표현의미예문
be able to R~할 수 있다She is able to speak French fluently.
be about to R막 ~하려는 참이다The movie is about to start.
be anxious to R~하기를 갈망하다He is anxious to hear the results.
be bound to R반드시 ~하다, ~할 운명이다You are bound to succeed if you work hard.
be eager to R~하기를 열망하다The children are eager to open their presents.
be forced/compelled to R~하도록 강요받다He was forced to resign from his position.
be likely to R~할 것 같다It is likely to rain this afternoon.
be ready to R~할 준비가 되다I am ready to begin the test.
be supposed to R~하기로 되어 있다, ~해야 한다You are supposed to finish this by 5 PM.
be willing to R기꺼이 ~하다She is willing to help anyone in need.
come to R~하게 되다I came to realize the importance of family.
fail to R~하지 못하다He failed to understand the instructions.
happen to R우연히 ~하다I happened to meet an old friend yesterday.
