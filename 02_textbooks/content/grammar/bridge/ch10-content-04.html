📚상세 예시

1. 의문대명사 (Who, Whom, Whose, What, Which) 심층 분석

  • who (주격: 누가): Who is responsible for this project? / Who ate the last piece of cake?
  • whom (목적격: 누구를, 누구에게): To whom should I send this package? / The man whom I respect is my father.
  • whose (소유격/소유대명사: 누구의 / 누구의 것):
    • Whose jacket is this? (의문형용사)
    • This pen is mine. Whose is that? (의문대명사)

2. 의문부사 (When, Where, Why, How) 심층 분석

특히 how는 다른 단어와 결합하여 다양한 의미를 만듭니다.

how long (기간/길이)How long will the meeting last?
how far (거리)How far is your home from here?
how often (빈도)How often do you go to the gym?
how many (+ 복수 가산 명사)How many students are in your class?
how much (+ 불가산 명사)How much sugar do you need?
how come (왜, 어째서 - 구어체)How come you didn't come to the party? (뒤에 평서문 어순)

3. 간접의문문 (Indirect Questions) 완벽 해설

간접의문문은 의문문이 다른 문장의 일부(주어, 목적어, 보어)로 사용되는 것을 말합니다. 핵심은 [의문사 + 주어 + 동사] 어순입니다.

  • I don't know what his job is. (직접: What is his job?)
  • (주의) `think, believe` 동사와 결합 시: Where do you think he is? (의문사가 문장 맨 앞으로 이동)
