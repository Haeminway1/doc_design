7.1. 감정 동사의 능동분사 vs. 수동분사 (추가 예시)

다양한 감정 동사의 분사 형태를 익혀두면 독해와 작문에서 문장의 뉘앙스를 정확하게 파악하고 표현할 수 있습니다.

-ing (감정 유발)-ed (감정 느낌)예문
embarrassing (당황스러운)embarrassed (당황한)It was an embarrassing situation. / He felt embarrassed.
frustrating (좌절감을 주는)frustrated (좌절한)The slow computer is frustrating. / She is frustrated with her progress.
overwhelming (압도적인)overwhelmed (압도된)The amount of work was overwhelming. / I feel overwhelmed.
touching (감동적인)touched (감동받은)The reunion was a touching moment. / We were touched by her kindness.
annoying (짜증나는)annoyed (짜증난)The constant noise is annoying. / I am annoyed by the noise.
depressing (우울하게 하는)depressed (우울한)The rainy weather can be depressing. / He has been feeling depressed lately.
shocking (충격적인)shocked (충격받은)The crime rate is shocking. / They were shocked to hear the news.
pleasing (기쁘게 하는)pleased (기쁜)The result is pleasing to the eye. / The manager was pleased with our work.
astonishing (정말 놀라운)astonished (크게 놀란)Her performance was astonishing. / I was astonished at her skills.
exhausting (진을 빼는)exhausted (기진맥진한)A marathon is an exhausting race. / The runners were exhausted.
