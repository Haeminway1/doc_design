2.3. 동명사의 시제와 태 변화

동명사는 동사의 성질을 가지므로, 문장의 본동사와의 시제 관계 및 태(능동/수동)를 표현할 수 있습니다.

능동태 (Active) 수동태 (Passive)
단순 동명사
(본동사와 시제 동일)		 V-ing
He is proud of winning the game.		 being + p.p.
He is proud of being loved by many people.
완료 동명사
(본동사보다 시제 앞섬)		 having + p.p.
He is proud of having won the game last year.		 having been + p.p.
He was ashamed of having been scolded by his teacher.
2.4. 동명사 주요 관용 표현
표현의미예문
cannot help ~ing~하지 않을 수 없다I cannot help laughing at his jokes.
be busy ~ing~하느라 바쁘다She is busy preparing for the presentation.
spend time/money (in) ~ing~하는 데 시간/돈을 쓰다He spent all day playing video games.
have difficulty/trouble (in) ~ing~하는 데 어려움을 겪다I had difficulty finding his house.
It is no use/good ~ing~해도 소용없다It is no use crying over spilt milk.
There is no ~ing~하는 것은 불가능하다There is no knowing what will happen.
feel like ~ing~하고 싶다I feel like eating pizza tonight.
go ~ing~하러 가다 (레저/스포츠)Let's go shopping this weekend.
