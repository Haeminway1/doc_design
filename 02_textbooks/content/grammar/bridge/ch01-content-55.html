1.2. 조동사의 핵심 의미와 뉘앙스 비교 (계속)

May / Might

  • 허가: You may begin your exam now. (`Can`보다 격식적)
  • 가능성/추측: It may rain this afternoon. (약 50% 가능성) / It might rain, but the sky is quite clear. (더 약한 가능성, 약 30% 이하)

Will / Would

  • 미래/의지: I will help you with your project. (강한 의지)
  • 요청: Will you close the door? (비격식) / Would you mind closing the door? (매우 정중)
  • 습관: He will often fall asleep during class. (현재의 불규칙적 습관/고집) / In my childhood, we would go to the beach every summer. (과거의 불규칙적 행동, `used to`와 유사)

Shall / Should

  • Shall: Shall we dance? (제안) / The defendant shall pay a fine. (법률/계약의 강한 의무)
  • Should: You should apologize to her. (충고/의무) / The package should arrive by Friday. (예상) / It is surprising that he should fail the exam. (감정적 판단, ~하다니)

Must

  • 강한 의무/필요: You must follow the rules. (선택의 여지 없음)
  • 강한 추측: She hasn't eaten all day. She must be hungry. (~임에 틀림없다)
  • 금지 (must not): You must not smoke in this building. (강한 금지)
