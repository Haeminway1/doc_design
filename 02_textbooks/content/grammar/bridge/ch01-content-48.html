Part 3 4형식 수동태 심화: 목적어 선택의 비밀
3.9.1. 간접목적어(I.O)와 직접목적어(D.O) 모두 주어 가능 동사

이 동사들은 두 목적어 모두 수동태의 주어가 될 수 있지만, 일반적으로 사람인 간접목적어(I.O)를 주어로 쓰는 것이 더 자연스럽습니다. 직접목적어(D.O)를 주어로 쓸 경우, 간접목적어 앞에 전치사(주로 to)가 붙습니다.

동사 예시 (I.O. 주어 - 더 자연스러움) 예시 (D.O. 주어)
giveI was given a book.A book was given to me.
sendHe was sent an email.An email was sent to him.
tellWe were told the truth.The truth was told to us.
showShe was shown the evidence.The evidence was shown to her.
teachThe students were taught English.English was taught to the students.
lendHe was lent some money.Some money was lent to him.
offerShe was offered a new job.A new job was offered to her.
grantThe refugee was granted asylum.Asylum was granted to the refugee.
awardThe winner was awarded a prize.A prize was awarded to the winner.
passHe was passed the salt.The salt was passed to him.
writeShe was written a long letter.A long letter was written to her.
promiseWe were promised a bonus.A bonus was promised to us.
payThe employee was paid a salary.A salary was paid to the employee.
sellThe customer was sold a faulty item.A faulty item was sold to the customer.
