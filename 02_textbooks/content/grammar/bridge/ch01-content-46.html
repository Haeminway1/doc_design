3.7.3. 자주 혼동되는 수동태 표현들

drown vs. be drowned

  • drown (능동): (물에 빠져) 익사하다. e.g., He slipped and drowned in the river.
  • be drowned (수동): (누군가에 의해) 익사당하다. e.g., The victim was drowned by the killer.

hang - hung - hung vs. hang - hanged - hanged

  • be hung: (물건이) 걸리다. e.g., The painting was hung on the wall.
  • be hanged: 교수형에 처해지다. e.g., The criminal was hanged for his crimes.

be seated vs. sit

  • be seated: 앉혀지다 (정중한 표현으로 '앉으세요'). e.g., Please be seated.
  • sit (자동사): (스스로) 앉다. 수동태로 쓰이지 않음. e.g., He sat on the chair.

expose to vs. be exposed to

  • expose A to B (능동): A를 B에 노출시키다. e.g., The scientist exposed the cells to radiation.
  • be exposed to (수동): ~에 노출되다. e.g., The film was exposed to light accidentally.

accustom to vs. be accustomed to

  • accustom oneself to (능동): ~에 익숙해지다 (동작). e.g., You must accustom yourself to the new rules.
  • be accustomed to (수동): ~에 익숙하다 (상태). e.g., He is accustomed to working long hours.

devote to vs. be devoted to

  • devote A to B (능동): A를 B에 바치다. e.g., She devoted her life to helping the poor.
  • be devoted to (수동): ~에 헌신하다 (상태). e.g., She is devoted to her children.

involve in vs. be involved in

  • involve A in B (능동): A를 B에 포함시키다/연루시키다. e.g., The project involved many departments in the company.
  • be involved in (수동): ~에 참여하다/관련되다. e.g., He is involved in several community activities.
