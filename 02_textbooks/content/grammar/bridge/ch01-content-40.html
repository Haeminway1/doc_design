5.2. 상태/소유 동사

일부 타동사라도 동작이 아닌 상태나 소유를 나타내는 동사는 수동태로 쓰지 않는 것이 일반적입니다.

동사올바른 형태 (능동)잘못된 수동태
have (소유)I have a car.A car is had by me.
belong toThis car belongs to me.This car is belonged to me.
resembleHe resembles his father.His father is resembled by him.
lackWe lack money.Money is lacked by us.
fit, suitThis dress fits me.I am fitted by this dress.
costThe book costs ten dollars.Ten dollars are cost by the book.
weigh (무게가 나가다)The bag weighs 5kg.5kg is weighed by the bag.
containThis bottle contains water.Water is contained by this bottle.
become (어울리다)That color becomes you.You are become by that color.
mean (의미하다)'No' means no.No is meant by 'No'.
escape (기억나지 않다)His name escapes me.I am escaped by his name.
