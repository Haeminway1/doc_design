Part 3 수동태 불가 동사들
3.5.1. 자동사 (Intransitive Verbs)

목적어가 없는 자동사는 수동태로 만들 수 없습니다. 우리말 해석에 속지 않도록 주의해야 합니다.

자동사올바른 형태 (능동)잘못된 수동태
happen, occur, take placeThe accident happened.The accident was happened.
appear, disappear, vanishHe appeared suddenly.He was appeared suddenly.
remain, exist, consist ofThe problem remained.The problem was remained.
result from/inThe fire resulted from carelessness.The fire was resulted from...
arrive, fall, riseHe arrived late.He was arrived late.
seem, look (보이다)She seemed happy.She was seemed happy.
last (지속되다)The meeting lasted two hours.The meeting was lasted two hours.
dieThe patient died peacefully.The patient was died peacefully.
work (작동하다)The machine works well.The machine is worked well.
emergeA new leader emerged.A new leader was emerged.
deteriorateHis condition deteriorated.His condition was deteriorated.
