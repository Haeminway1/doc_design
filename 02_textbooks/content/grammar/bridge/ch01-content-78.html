주요 구두점 핵심 용법 비교
구두점핵심 기능대표 용법 및 예시
콤마 (,)짧은 쉼 / 구분
(Separation)		 ① 나열: `I need to buy apples, bananas, and milk.`
② 접속사 연결: `She studied hard, so she passed the test.`
③ 도입구 뒤: `After finishing his homework, he watched TV.`
세미콜론 (;)긴밀한 연결
(Connection)		 ① 독립절 연결: `The sun is shining; the birds are singing.`
② 접속부사: `He felt ill; therefore, he went home.`
③ 복잡한 나열: `I visited Seoul, Korea; Tokyo, Japan; and Beijing, China.`
콜론 (:)소개 / 예고
(Introduction)		 ① 목록/설명: `You have two choices: finish the work today or do it tomorrow.`
② 인용: `The sign read: "Keep Out."`
대시 (—)강조 / 중단
(Emphasis)		 ① 부가 설명/강조: `My favorite color—the one I paint with—is blue.`
② 갑작스러운 전환: `I was about to leave—and then I realized I had the wrong date.`
