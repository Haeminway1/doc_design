11.2.4. 동명사 주요 관용 표현
표현의미예문
look forward to ~ing~을 고대하다 (to는 전치사)I'm looking forward to seeing you again.
be used/accustomed to ~ing~에 익숙하다 (to는 전치사)He is used to waking up early.
object to ~ing~에 반대하다 (to는 전치사)My parents object to my marrying him.
What do you say to ~ing?~하는 게 어때?What do you say to having dinner together?
on ~ing~하자마자On arriving home, I turned on the TV.
prevent/stop/keep A from ~ingA가 ~하는 것을 막다The heavy rain kept us from going out.
be worth ~ing~할 가치가 있다This book is worth reading several times.
make a point of ~ing반드시 ~하다I make a point of exercising every morning.
contribute to ~ing~에 기여/공헌하다Regular exercise contributes to maintaining good health.
devote A to ~ingA를 ~에 바치다, 헌신하다She devoted her life to helping the poor.
be on the point of ~ing막 ~하려는 참이다He was on the point of leaving when the phone rang.
come near/close to ~ing거의 ~할 뻔하다The car came near hitting the child.
