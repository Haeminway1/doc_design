3.9.2. 직접목적어(D.O)만 주어 가능 동사

이 동사들은 '~를 위해'라는 의미를 가지는 for와 함께 3형식으로 전환되는 경우가 많습니다. 이 동사들은 수동태로 전환될 때 사물인 직접목적어(D.O)만 주어로 사용할 수 있습니다. 사람인 간접목적어를 주어로 쓰면 매우 어색하거나 문법적으로 틀린 문장이 됩니다.

동사 올바른 수동태 (D.O. 주어) 틀린 수동태 (I.O. 주어)
buyA gift was bought for me.I was bought a gift.
makeA cake was made for her.She was made a cake.
cookDinner was cooked for us.We were cooked dinner.
getA drink was gotten for him.He was gotten a drink.
findA good seat was found for me.I was found a good seat.
buildA house was built for the family.The family was built a house.
chooseA dress was chosen for her.She was chosen a dress.
orderA pizza was ordered for them.They were ordered a pizza.
singA song was sung for the audience.The audience was sung a song.
playA tune was played for her.She was played a tune.
prepareA meal was prepared for the guests.The guests were prepared a meal.
designA logo was designed for the company.The company was designed a logo.
reserveA table was reserved for us.We were reserved a table.
saveA seat was saved for me.I was saved a seat.
