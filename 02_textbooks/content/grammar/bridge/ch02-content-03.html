📊핵심 가정법 구문 및 도치 (심화)

1. 주요 가정법 공식 심화

종류 If절 (조건절) 주절 (결과절) 핵심 의미
가정법 과거 If + S + 과거동사/were... S + would/could/might + R 현재 사실과
반대되는 상상
If the government implemented a more effective policy, the unemployment rate could decrease significantly.
가정법 과거완료 If + S + had p.p.... S + would/could/might + have p.p. 과거 사실에 대한
후회/반대 상상
If the team had analyzed the market trends more carefully, they might not have launched the unsuccessful product.
혼합 가정법 If + S + had p.p.... S + would/could/might + R 과거의 원인이
현재에 미치는 영향
If she had accepted the job offer last year, she would be working as a senior manager now.
가정법 미래 If + S + should/were to + R... S + will/would/should + R 실현 희박한
미래 가정
If you should encounter any issues...
If the sun were to rise in the west...		 please contact our support team.
I would not change my mind.
