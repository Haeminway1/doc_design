9.2. 직접목적어(D.O)만 주어 가능 동사 (추가 예시)

아래는 직접목적어(D.O)만을 수동태 주어로 사용하는 추가적인 동사 목록입니다. 이 동사들의 공통점은 '수혜자를 위한 행위'라는 뉘앙스를 강하게 풍긴다는 것입니다.

동사 올바른 수동태 (D.O. 주어) 틀린 수동태 (I.O. 주어)
leaveA message was left for you.You were left a message.
fetchA ball was fetched for the dog.The dog was fetched a ball.
pourA glass of wine was poured for him.He was poured a glass of wine.
cashA check was cashed for my grandmother.My grandmother was cashed a check.
sewA new uniform was sewn for the player.The player was sewn a new uniform.
knitA sweater was knitted for the baby.The baby was knitted a sweater.
paintA portrait was painted for the queen.The queen was painted a portrait.
bookA flight was booked for the manager.The manager was booked a flight.
fixA sandwich was fixed for me.I was fixed a sandwich.
peelAn orange was peeled for the child.The child was peeled an orange.
carveA sculpture was carved for the city.The city was carved a sculpture.
Vera's Flavor Tip: 4형식 수동태의 함정!

동사가 '~를 위해 ~해주다'라는 의미(buy, make, cook 등)를 가질 때는, '사람'이 아닌 '사물'만 수동태의 주어가 될 수 있다는 점을 반드시 기억하세요. 이것이 편입 시험에서 자주 나오는 함정 포인트입니다!
