🔥 단원 마무리 테스트

  1. 빈칸에 가장 적절한 것을 선택하시오.
    The documentary was so _______ that many viewers were moved to tears.
    ① moved ② moving ③ move ④ to move
  2. 빈칸에 가장 적절한 것을 선택하시오.
    Rather than _______ about the problem, we should focus on _______ solutions.
    ① complaining, finding ② complain, find ③ to complain, to find ④ complained, found
  3. 빈칸에 가장 적절한 것을 선택하시오.
    The machine needs _______ before it can be used safely.
    ① to repair ② repairing ③ repaired ④ repair
  4. 빈칸에 가장 적절한 것을 선택하시오.
    _______ the instructions carefully will help you avoid _______ mistakes.
    ① Read, make ② Reading, making ③ To read, to make ④ Having read, made
  5. 빈칸에 가장 적절한 것을 선택하시오.
    The committee decided _______ the meeting until next week due to _______ circumstances.
    ① postpone, unforeseen ② to postpone, unforeseen ③ postponing, unforseeable ④ to postpone, unforeseeing
  6. 다음 문장에서 어법상 틀린 부분을 찾아 바르게 고치시오.
    Being a successful entrepreneur requires to take risks and to be persistence in the face of challenges.
  7. 다음 문장에서 어법상 틀린 부분을 찾아 바르게 고치시오.
    The research conducted by the team last year showed that regular exercise not only improves physical health but also enhancing mental well-being.
  8. 다음 문장에서 어법상 틀린 부분을 찾아 바르게 고치시오.
    Many students find the concept of quantum mechanics fascinated, despite it being one of the most confusing topics in physics.
