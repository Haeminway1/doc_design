Testing Point 20: do 동사의 여러 쓰임

전체 기출 빈도: ★★★☆☆ 최근 기출 빈도: ★★★★☆

핵심 개념: `do/does/did`는 앞에 나온 일반동사의 반복을 피하기 위한 대동사로 쓰이거나, 동사의 의미를 강조할 때, 또는 도치 구문에서 사용됩니다.

주요 쓰임

대동사: 일반동사구의 반복을 피하기 위해 사용. (e.g., She plays tennis better now than she did last month.)

강조: 동사의 의미를 강조. (e.g., Germans do like to discuss things a lot.)

도치: 부정어구가 문두에 올 때 조동사로 사용. (e.g., Not only does God play dice...)

예제 분석: Looking back, I learned more from my failures than I was / did from my successes.

1. 반복되는 동사구 확인: `learned ... from my successes`가 반복됨.
2. 동사 종류 파악: `learned`는 일반동사.
3. 대동사 선택: 일반동사를 대신하고 시제가 과거이므로 did가 정답.

Point Exercise

  1. There are some women who feel the same parental love for other children as they do / are for their own.
  2. As civilizations developed, so did / were fashions.
  3. We do not hear with our eyes, but sometimes it almost seems as if we do / are.
  4. "Monumental" is a word that comes very close to expressing the basic characteristic of Egyptian art. Never before and never since has the quality of monumentality been achieved as fully as it did / was in Egypt.
  5. Those who give small amounts to many charities are not so interested in whether what they are doing / being helps others.
