⚠️ 함정 패턴 완전 분석

함정 1: 삽입절 혼동 (관계대명사 + I think류)
The person who (I think) is responsible... → `I think`를 빼면 주격 `who`가 필요함을 알 수 있음.

함정 2: 전치사 + 관계대명사
`전치사 + whom/which`는 가능하지만, `전치사 + that/who`는 불가능합니다.
The pen with which I wrote... (O) / The pen with that I wrote... (X)

함정 3: 계속적 용법(콤마 뒤) 제한
콤마(,) 뒤의 계속적 용법으로는 `that`과 `what`을 사용할 수 없습니다.
He failed the exam, which disappointed his parents. (O) / ..., that disappointed... (X)

연습 문제 (1/3)

  1. The scientist whom / who discovered the vaccine received international recognition.
  2. The research which / whose results were published last week contradicts previous findings.
  3. This is the hospital which / where my grandmother was treated successfully.
  4. The reason which / why he resigned remains unclear to many colleagues.
  5. The committee members, most of whom / which are experts, will review the proposals.
