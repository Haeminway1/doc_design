연습 문제 (1/3)

  1. This restaurant serves food that is as good as / better than / the best any restaurant in the city.
  2. The more / most you practice, the good / better / best you become at playing the piano.
  3. She is intelligent / more intelligent / the most intelligent student in the entire graduating class.
  4. The weather today is much / very cold / colder / coldest than it was yesterday morning.
  5. This is by far / very the good / the better / the best movie I have seen this year.
  6. He speaks English as fluent / as fluently / more fluently as a native speaker would.
  7. The new model is slightly / quite expensive / more expensive than the previous version.
  8. Among all the candidates, she performed good / better / the best during the interview process.
  9. This problem is much / very easy / easier / easiest to solve than the previous one.
