연습 문제 (1/2)

  1. She graduated / has graduated from university three years ago.
  2. We lived / have lived in this city since 2015.
  3. The company was founded / has been founded in 1990 by two brothers.
  4. I saw / have seen that movie last weekend with my friends.
  5. Technology changed / has changed dramatically over the last few decades.
  6. When did you finish / have you finished your project?
  7. He worked / has worked as a teacher for 20 years before he retired.
  8. So far, the team won / has won all of its matches this season.
  9. Shakespeare wrote / has written many famous plays and sonnets.
