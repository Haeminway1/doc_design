연습 문제 (1/2)

  1. There are two books on the table. One is mine, and another / the other is yours.
  2. Some people prefer coffee, while other / others prefer tea.
  3. I don't like this shirt. Could you show me another / the other one?
  4. He has three sons. One is a doctor, another / the other is a lawyer, and another / the other is an artist.
  5. This plan has some problems. We need to find another / other solutions.
  6. Of the ten applicants, three were hired. What happened to other / the other seven?
  7. One of my hobbies is reading; another / the other is hiking in the mountains.
  8. You've finished this task. Now you can move on to another / the other.
  9. Some students finished the exam early, while the others / others needed more time.
