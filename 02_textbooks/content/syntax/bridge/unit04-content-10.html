🔥 단원 마무리 테스트

  1. The hospital ⓐwhere my grandmother ⓑwas treated successfully ⓒis located ⓓin which the city center. (틀린 것 찾기)
  2. ⓐWhat I find most ⓑinteresting about this research is ⓒwhat it challenges ⓓconventional wisdom completely. (틀린 것 찾기)
  3. ⓐBecause of the heavy snowfall, ⓑmany flights were cancelled, ⓒwhich caused ⓓsignificant delays for travelers. (틀린 것 찾기)
  4. The professor ⓐwhose research ⓑfocuses on artificial intelligence ⓒwas invited to speak ⓓat that the conference. (틀린 것 찾기)
  5. ⓐDuring the meeting ⓑwas taking place, several important ⓒdecisions were ⓓmade regarding the project. (틀린 것 찾기)
  6. The moment _______ I saw the accident, I immediately called for emergency assistance.
    ① when ② which ③ that ④ where
  7. _______ the economic recession, many companies had to reduce their workforce significantly.
    ① Despite ② Although ③ Because of ④ Since
  8. The building _______ the famous architect designed has become a landmark of the city.
    ① where ② which ③ when ④ why
  9. _______ concerns me most is _______ students are losing interest in reading books.
    ① What, that ② That, what ③ What, what ④ That, that
  10. The conference room _______ can accommodate up to 200 people will be used for the presentation.
    ① where ② which ③ when ④ whose
