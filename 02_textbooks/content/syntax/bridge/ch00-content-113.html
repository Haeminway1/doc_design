🔥 단원 마무리 테스트 (2/2)

  1. 다음 문장에서 어법상 틀린 부분을 찾아 바르게 고치시오.
    One of the most common mistakes made by learners are confusing similar-sounding words.
  2. 다음 문장에서 어법상 틀린 부분을 찾아 바르게 고치시오.
    Never before I have seen such a beautiful sunset.
  3. 다음 문장에서 어법상 틀린 부분을 찾아 바르게 고치시오.
    She regrets not to study harder for the exam she took last week.
  4. 다음 문장에서 어법상 틀린 부분을 찾아 바르게 고치시오.
    The package I have sent two days ago has not arrived yet.
  5. 다음 문장에서 어법상 틀린 부분을 찾아 바르게 고치시오.
    It is essential that every employee follows the safety regulations.
  6. 빈칸에 가장 적절한 것을 선택하시오.
    Half of the apples in the basket _______ rotten.
    ① is ② are ③ has ④ have
  7. 빈칸에 가장 적절한 것을 선택하시오.
    If he _______ more careful, the accident would not have happened.
    ① was ② were ③ had been ④ has been
  8. 빈칸에 가장 적절한 것을 선택하시오.
    There _______ a number of issues that need to be addressed.
    ① is ② are ③ was ④ were
  9. 다음 문장에서 어법상 틀린 부분을 찾아 바르게 고치시오.
    All the furniture in this room look old and need to be replaced.
  10. 다음 문장에서 어법상 틀린 부분을 찾아 바르게 고치시오.
    The team is working hard, and so is every member of the staff.
