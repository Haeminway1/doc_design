Challenge 3: A New Look
본문

①Of all the challenges I chose for myself, the third one made me the most nervous: changing my hairstyle. ②For many years, I had kept the same hairstyle because I had been worried about my forehead. ③I had always thought it was large, and I didn't want to draw attention to it. ④Since I was feeling confident after the first two challenges, I went to a hair salon and talked to the stylist. ⑤He listened to my concerns and helped me find a shorter hairstyle that suited me.

⑥The next day at school, my classmates were a bit surprised. ⑦But overall, the reactions were positive. ⑧I realized that trying a different look wasn't as difficult as I thought it would be. ⑨In fact, it made me feel more confident about myself. ⑩I didn't expect that stepping out of my comfort zone would have such a positive effect on how I felt about myself.

문장별 해석
①
Of all the challenges I chose for myself, the third one made me the most nervous: changing my hairstyle.
내가 스스로 선택한 모든 도전 중에서, 세 번째 것이 나를 가장 긴장되게 했다: 헤어스타일을 바꾸는 것.
②
For many years, I had kept the same hairstyle because I had been worried about my forehead.
여러 해 동안, 이마가 걱정되어서 같은 헤어스타일을 유지해 왔다.
③
I had always thought it was large, and I didn't want to draw attention to it.
나는 항상 이마가 크다고 생각했고, 이마에 시선이 가는 것을 원하지 않았다.
④
Since I was feeling confident after the first two challenges, I went to a hair salon and talked to the stylist.
처음 두 도전 후에 자신감이 생겼기 때문에, 미용실에 가서 헤어 디자이너에게 말했다.
⑤
He listened to my concerns and helped me find a shorter hairstyle that suited me.
그는 나의 고민을 들어주었고 나에게 어울리는 더 짧은 헤어스타일을 찾도록 도와주었다.
⑥
The next day at school, my classmates were a bit surprised.
다음 날 학교에서, 급우들은 약간 놀랐다.
⑦
But overall, the reactions were positive.
하지만 전반적으로, 반응은 긍정적이었다.
⑧
I realized that trying a different look wasn't as difficult as I thought it would be.
나는 다른 외모를 시도하는 것이 내가 생각했던 것만큼 어렵지 않다는 것을 깨달았다.
⑨
In fact, it made me feel more confident about myself.
사실, 그것은 나 자신에 대해 더 자신감을 갖게 해 주었다.
⑩
I didn't expect that stepping out of my comfort zone would have such a positive effect on how I felt about myself.
안전지대에서 벗어나는 것이 내가 나 자신에 대해 느끼는 방식에 그렇게 긍정적인 영향을 미칠 줄은 예상하지 못했다.
