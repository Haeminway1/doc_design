Discovering Yourself through Challenges — 구문독해
① "To grow into a better version of ourselves, we should not stay as we are."
to부정사 To grow into ~ : 부사적 용법(목적) — '~으로 성장하기 위해'
접속사 as we are : '현재 있는 그대로'. stay as S + V = '~인 상태로 머물다'
② That was the first thing our teacher said to us on the first day of school.
관계대명사 the first thing (that) : 목적격 관계대명사 that 생략. '선생님이 말한 첫 번째 것'
표현 say to + 사람 : ~에게 말하다
③ She wanted us to start the semester by pushing ourselves out of our comfort zones and learning more about ourselves.
5형식 want + O + to-V : '(목적어)가 ~하기를 원하다'
전치사 by + V-ing : 수단/방법 — '~함으로써'
표현 push oneself out of ~ : ~에서 벗어나다 / comfort zone : 안전지대
병렬 pushing ~ and learning ~ : by의 목적어로 두 동명사구 병렬
④ To help us do so, she gave us an assignment to challenge ourselves in three different ways.
to부정사 To help us do so : 부사적 용법(목적) — '우리가 그렇게 하는 것을 돕기 위해'
5형식 help + O + (to) V : 원형부정사 허용. do so : 앞 내용을 받는 대동사
to부정사 an assignment to challenge : to부정사 형용사적 용법 — assignment를 수식
⑤ The three challenges could involve hobbies, exercise, our studies, or even our relationships.
조동사 could : 가능성 — '~할 수도 있었다'
표현 or even : '심지어 ~도' — 나열 항목 강조
⑥ The important part was trying something new for each one.
동명사 trying ~ : 동명사구가 주격보어. 'S = trying ~' 구조
어순 something new : -thing/-body/-one으로 끝나는 대명사는 형용사가 뒤에서 수식
⑦ She told us that we should share our experiences with our classmates when we finished the challenges.
4형식 tell + IO + that절 : '(사람)에게 ~라고 말하다'
표현 share A with B : A를 B와 공유하다
부사절 when we finished : 시간 부사절 — '~했을 때'
