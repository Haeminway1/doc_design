Challenge 2: Riding the Waves
본문

①Ever since I was a child, I've always been afraid of water. ②So I have never enjoyed water activities or felt comfortable while doing them. ③For the second challenge, I decided to try to overcome this discomfort. ④However, I wasn't sure what to do at first. ⑤One day, I came across a video clip of my cousin surfing at an indoor pool on social media. ⑥It looked fun, and I liked the fact that it took place in a shallow pool, not in the ocean. ⑦I messaged my cousin about it. ⑧She assured me that it wasn't too hard, so I signed up for a beginner course.

⑨At the beginning of the lesson, it was hard just to stand up on the board. ⑩After some help from the instructor, however, I learned how to balance properly and even managed to surf a few waves! ⑪It gave me such a rush of excitement. ⑫Being in the water wasn't as bad as I had thought. ⑬While the experience didn't completely change my mind about water sports, it did help me discover my sense of adventure.

문장별 해석
①
Ever since I was a child, I've always been afraid of water.
어릴 때부터, 나는 항상 물을 무서워했다.
②
So I have never enjoyed water activities or felt comfortable while doing them.
그래서 나는 수상 활동을 즐기거나 그것을 하면서 편안함을 느낀 적이 없었다.
③
For the second challenge, I decided to try to overcome this discomfort.
두 번째 도전으로, 나는 이 불편함을 극복하기로 결심했다.
④
However, I wasn't sure what to do at first.
하지만, 처음에는 무엇을 해야 할지 확신이 없었다.
⑤
One day, I came across a video clip of my cousin surfing at an indoor pool on social media.
어느 날, 나는 소셜 미디어에서 사촌이 실내 수영장에서 서핑하는 영상을 우연히 발견했다.
⑥
It looked fun, and I liked the fact that it took place in a shallow pool, not in the ocean.
그것은 재미있어 보였고, 바다가 아닌 얕은 수영장에서 이루어진다는 사실이 마음에 들었다.
⑦
I messaged my cousin about it.
나는 사촌에게 그것에 대해 메시지를 보냈다.
⑧
She assured me that it wasn't too hard, so I signed up for a beginner course.
그녀는 그것이 너무 어렵지 않다고 확신시켜 주었고, 그래서 나는 초급 과정에 등록했다.
⑨
At the beginning of the lesson, it was hard just to stand up on the board.
수업 초반에는, 보드 위에 서는 것조차 힘들었다.
⑩
After some help from the instructor, however, I learned how to balance properly and even managed to surf a few waves!
하지만 강사의 도움을 받은 후, 나는 균형을 잡는 법을 배웠고 심지어 몇 개의 파도를 타는 데 성공했다!
⑪
It gave me such a rush of excitement.
그것은 나에게 엄청난 흥분의 전율을 주었다.
⑫
Being in the water wasn't as bad as I had thought.
물속에 있는 것은 내가 생각했던 것만큼 나쁘지 않았다.
⑬
While the experience didn't completely change my mind about water sports, it did help me discover my sense of adventure.
그 경험이 수상 스포츠에 대한 나의 생각을 완전히 바꾸지는 않았지만, 그것은 내 모험심을 발견하도록 도와주었다.
