마무리 — 구문독해
① I learned about myself through this assignment, and I found out that my classmates did, too.
표현 learn about ~ : ~에 대해 배우다
표현 find out that ~ : ~임을 알게 되다
대동사 did (too) : 앞의 learned a lot about myself를 대신하는 대동사 did
② One of them tried new foods from different countries, while another joined a creative writing club.
표현 One of them : 그들 중 한 명. 단수 취급
접속사 while : 대조 — '반면에'. One ~ while another ~ : '한 명은 ~하고, 또 다른 한 명은 ~하다'
③ We all realized how important it is to push ourselves to try new things.
간접의문문 how important it is ~ : 의문사절(how + 형용사 + S + V)이 realized의 목적어
가주어-진주어 it is ~ to push : it은 가주어, to push ourselves to try new things가 진주어
5형식 push + O + to-V : '(목적어)가 ~하도록 밀어붙이다'
④ We gained different perspectives on life and had a lot of fun, too!
어휘 gain : 얻다, 획득하다
표현 perspectives on ~ : ~에 대한 관점. gain perspectives on life : 삶에 대한 관점을 얻다
⑤ My classmates and I even talked about setting more challenges for ourselves.
전치사 + 동명사 talk about V-ing : 전치사 about 뒤에 동명사구가 목적어로 옴
⑥ There are always more things that we can discover about ourselves, and it's exciting to keep learning and growing.
관계대명사 things that we can discover : 목적격 관계대명사 that. things를 선행사로 수식
가주어-진주어 it's exciting to keep ~ : it은 가주어, to keep learning and growing이 진주어
표현 keep V-ing : 계속 ~하다
⑦ We never know what we're capable of until we try!
간접의문문 what we're capable of : 의문사 + S + V 어순. know의 목적어절
표현 be capable of ~ : ~을 할 수 있다, ~의 능력이 있다
접속사 not ~ until … : …하기 전까지는 ~하지 않다 → '…해야 비로소 ~하다'
