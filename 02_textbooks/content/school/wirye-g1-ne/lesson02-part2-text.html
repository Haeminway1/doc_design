Talk It Out, Help Is Here — Part 2
본문

Host ①What a wonderful idea! ②How did these amazing grandmothers become a part of your program?

Dr. Chibanda ③Grandmothers were chosen for our program because they are deeply connected to the community. ④Moreover, they have all the qualities of a counselor, such as excellent listening skills, empathy, and the ability to engage in meaningful discussions. ⑤They are also very reliable, which is important for our efforts to support mental health. ⑥At the beginning, we started with only fourteen volunteer grandmothers. ⑦But today, there are thousands of grandmothers who have been trained to provide Friendship Bench therapy in Zimbabwe.

Host ⑧That's interesting! ⑨What happens after patients complete their sessions with the grandmothers?

Dr. Chibanda ⑩When the sessions with the grandmothers are over, patients are encouraged to join a community support group made up of former patients of the program. ⑪The members of these groups can easily relate to and support one another. ⑫This is because most of them come from the same community and have experienced similar struggles.

Host ⑬That sounds like a great way to continue the support and build a sense of community. ⑭And the program has been incredibly successful, right?

Dr. Chibanda ⑮That's correct. ⑯In 2022, we provided face-to-face counseling to about 90,000 patients in Zimbabwe. ⑰Now, Friendship Benches are available in more than twenty different regions around the country. ⑱Our vision is for everyone to have a Friendship Bench within walking distance, and we are working hard to make it happen. ⑲We've also started expanding the program, and we now offer online counseling through an app. ⑳This has helped us reach even more people.

①
What a wonderful idea!

②
How did these amazing grandmothers become a part of your program?
이 놀라운 할머니들은 어떻게 프로그램의 일원이 되셨나요?
③
Grandmothers were chosen for our program because they are deeply connected to the community.
할머니들은 지역사회와 깊이 연결되어 있기 때문에 우리 프로그램을 위해 선발되었습니다.
④
Moreover, they have all the qualities of a counselor, such as excellent listening skills, empathy, and the ability to engage in meaningful discussions.
게다가, 그들은 뛰어난 경청 능력, 공감 능력, 그리고 의미 있는 토론에 참여하는 능력과 같은 상담사의 모든 자질을 갖추고 있습니다.
⑤
They are also very reliable, which is important for our efforts to support mental health.
그들은 또한 매우 신뢰할 수 있는데, 이것은 정신 건강을 지원하려는 우리의 노력에 중요합니다.
⑥
At the beginning, we started with only fourteen volunteer grandmothers.
처음에, 우리는 단 14명의 자원봉사 할머니로 시작했습니다.
⑦
But today, there are thousands of grandmothers who have been trained to provide Friendship Bench therapy in Zimbabwe.
하지만 오늘날, 짐바브웨에서 우정 벤치 치료를 제공하도록 훈련받은 수천 명의 할머니가 있습니다.
⑧
That's interesting!
흥미롭네요!
⑨
What happens after patients complete their sessions with the grandmothers?
환자들이 할머니들과의 세션을 마친 후에는 어떻게 되나요?
⑩
When the sessions with the grandmothers are over, patients are encouraged to join a community support group made up of former patients of the program.
할머니들과의 세션이 끝나면, 환자들은 프로그램의 이전 환자들로 구성된 지역사회 지원 그룹에 참여하도록 권장됩니다.
⑪
The members of these groups can easily relate to and support one another.
이 그룹의 구성원들은 서로 쉽게 공감하고 지지할 수 있습니다.
⑫
This is because most of them come from the same community and have experienced similar struggles.
이것은 그들 대부분이 같은 지역사회 출신이고 비슷한 어려움을 경험했기 때문입니다.
⑬
That sounds like a great way to continue the support and build a sense of community.
지원을 계속하고 공동체 의식을 형성하는 훌륭한 방법처럼 들리네요.
⑭
And the program has been incredibly successful, right?
그리고 프로그램이 엄청나게 성공적이었죠, 그렇죠?
⑮
That's correct.
맞습니다.
⑯
In 2022, we provided face-to-face counseling to about 90,000 patients in Zimbabwe.
2022년에, 우리는 짐바브웨에서 약 9만 명의 환자에게 대면 상담을 제공했습니다.
⑰
Now, Friendship Benches are available in more than twenty different regions around the country.
현재, 우정 벤치는 전국 20개 이상의 다른 지역에서 이용할 수 있습니다.
⑱
Our vision is for everyone to have a Friendship Bench within walking distance, and we are working hard to make it happen.
우리의 비전은 모든 사람이 걸어서 갈 수 있는 거리에 우정 벤치를 갖는 것이며, 우리는 그것을 실현하기 위해 열심히 노력하고 있습니다.
⑲
We've also started expanding the program, and we now offer online counseling through an app.
우리는 또한 프로그램을 확장하기 시작했고, 이제 앱을 통해 온라인 상담을 제공합니다.
⑳
This has helped us reach even more people.
이것은 우리가 더 많은 사람들에게 다가가는 데 도움이 되었습니다.
