Part 2 — 구문독해
① What a + 형용사 + 명사 감탄문
What a wonderful idea!
What a + 형용사 + 명사(+ 주어 + 동사)! — 명사를 강조하는 감탄문 형식. What a wonderful idea!에서 주어·동사가 생략된 구어체 감탄문
② How did ~ become 의문사 의문문
How did these amazing grandmothers become a part of your program?
How did + S + V원형 — 방법·경위를 묻는 의문사 의문문. become a part of: '~의 일원[일부]이 되다'
③ 수동태 이유부사절 because
Grandmothers were chosen for our program because they are deeply connected to the community.
were chosen: be동사 + 과거분사 수동태 — '선발되었다'. because S+V: 이유를 나타내는 부사절. be connected to: '~와 연결되어 있다'
④ Moreover such as A, B, and C
Moreover, they have all the qualities of a counselor, such as excellent listening skills, empathy, and the ability to engage in meaningful discussions.
Moreover: 담화표지 '게다가' — 앞 내용에 정보를 추가. the qualities of: '~의 자질'. such as A, B, and C: 병렬 나열(예시 열거). the ability to engage: to부정사의 형용사적 용법 — ability를 수식
⑤ 계속적 관계대명사 efforts to V
They are also very reliable, which is important for our efforts to support mental health.
which is important: 계속적(비제한적) 관계대명사 — which가 앞 절 전체를 받아 부연 설명. efforts to support: to부정사의 형용사적 용법 — efforts를 수식('지원하려는 노력')
⑥ At the beginning start with
At the beginning, we started with only fourteen volunteer grandmothers.
At the beginning: '처음에' (시간 부사구). start with: '~로 시작하다' — with 뒤에 출발점·재료를 나타내는 명사구
⑦ thousands of 관계대명사 + 현재완료 수동
there are thousands of grandmothers who have been trained to provide Friendship Bench therapy in Zimbabwe.
thousands of: '수천의' (막연한 복수를 나타내는 표현). who have been trained: 관계대명사절 내 현재완료 수동태 — '훈련을 받아온'(계속·완료)
⑧ 단순 감탄
That's interesting!
That's interesting! — 구어체 반응 표현. interesting: 현재분사형 형용사 '흥미로운'
⑨ What happens after S+V 시간부사절
What happens after patients complete their sessions with the grandmothers?
What happens after S+V: '~한 후에 어떻게 되나요?' — after절이 시간 부사절 역할. 부사절 내에서는 현재시제(complete)로 미래 의미 표현
⑩ 수동태 + to부정사 made up of
patients are encouraged to join a community support group made up of former patients of the program.
are encouraged to V: 수동태 + to부정사 — '~하도록 권장된다'. made up of: '~로 구성된' (과거분사구 — group을 뒤에서 수식)
⑪ relate to one another
The members of these groups can easily relate to and support one another.
relate to: '~에 공감하다, 이해하다'. one another: '서로' — 셋 이상의 상호 관계를 나타내는 대명사(each other와 혼용 가능)
⑫ This is because S+V 현재완료(경험)
This is because most of them come from the same community and have experienced similar struggles.
This is because S+V: '이것은 ~때문이다' — 앞 문장의 원인·이유를 설명. have experienced: 현재완료(경험) — 과거에서 현재에 이르는 경험을 나타냄
⑬ sounds like a way to V
That sounds like a great way to continue the support and build a sense of community.
sounds like + 명사구: '~처럼 들리다'. a way to continue: to부정사의 형용사적 용법 — way를 수식('계속하는 방법')
⑭ 현재완료(계속) incredibly
the program has been incredibly successful, right?
has been successful: 현재완료(계속) — 과거부터 현재까지 성공적인 상태가 지속됨. incredibly: '엄청나게, 믿을 수 없을 만큼' (형용사 successful을 수식하는 부사)
⑮ 단순 응답
That's correct.
That's correct. — 상대방의 말을 확인·동의하는 구어 표현
⑯ provided A to B face-to-face
we provided face-to-face counseling to about 90,000 patients in Zimbabwe.
provide A to B: 'B에게 A를 제공하다' (3형식 — provide B with A로 바꿔 쓸 수 있음). face-to-face: '대면의, 직접 만나는' (복합 형용사)
⑰ are available in more than
Friendship Benches are available in more than twenty different regions around the country.
are available in: '~에서 이용[구입] 가능하다'. more than + 수사: '~이상, ~을 초과하는'
⑱ for + O + to V (의미상 주어) make it happen
Our vision is for everyone to have a Friendship Bench within walking distance, and we are working hard to make it happen.
for + O + to V: to부정사의 의미상 주어 — 'everyone이 갖는 것'. within walking distance: '도보 거리 내에'. make it happen: 사역동사 make + 목적어 it + 동사원형 happen — '그것이 실현되게 하다'
⑲ have started V-ing offer A through B
We've also started expanding the program, and we now offer online counseling through an app.
have started expanding: start + V-ing — '~하기 시작했다' (동명사 목적어). 현재완료로 최근까지 이어지는 행위 강조. offer A through B: 'B를 통해 A를 제공하다'
⑳ help + O + V원형 현재완료(결과)
This has helped us reach even more people.
help + O + V원형: 'O가 ~하는 것을 돕다' — 목적격보어로 동사원형(또는 to부정사) 사용. has helped: 현재완료(결과) — 과거 행위의 결과가 현재에 미침
