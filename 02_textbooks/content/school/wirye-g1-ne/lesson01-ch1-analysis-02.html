핵심 표현
표현 의미 본문 활용
tend to V ~하는 경향이 있다, ~하는 편이다 I tend to watch movies on streaming services.
give it a try 한번 해보다 I decided to give it a try.
on my own 스스로, 혼자서 choose the movie and the seat on my own
see A as B A를 B로 보다[여기다] I saw the experience as a good opportunity
become absorbed in ~에 빠져들다, ~에 몰입하다 I became completely absorbed in the story.
nothing to V ~할 것이 아무것도 없다 There was nothing to distract me!
make + O + V원형 O가 ~하게 하다 (사역) This experience made me realize that ...
promise oneself that ~하겠다고 (스스로) 다짐하다 I promised myself that I would try ...
by myself 혼자서, 스스로 sit in the theater by myself / enjoy activities by myself
