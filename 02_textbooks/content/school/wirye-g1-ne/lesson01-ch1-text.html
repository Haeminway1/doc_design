Challenge 1: Ticket for One
본문

①These days, I tend to watch movies on streaming services. ②However, if I go to the theater, I usually watch an action or science fiction movie with my friends or family. ③Those are the kinds of movies they enjoy.

④Going to the theater alone was something I had never done before. ⑤So, for the first challenge, I decided to give it a try. ⑥It was nice to choose the movie and the seat on my own. ⑦I didn't have to worry about anyone else's preferences. ⑧I saw the experience as a good opportunity to watch something different, so I chose a historical drama that I had been curious about.

⑨At first, it felt a bit awkward and lonely to sit in the theater by myself. ⑩But once the movie started, I became completely absorbed in the story. ⑪It was so satisfying just to focus on the movie itself. ⑫There was nothing to distract me! ⑬This experience made me realize that I sometimes enjoy doing things alone. ⑭After the movie, I promised myself that I would try to make my own choices more often and enjoy more activities by myself in the future.

문장별 해석
①
These days, I tend to watch movies on streaming services.
요즘 나는 스트리밍 서비스로 영화를 보는 편이다.
②
However, if I go to the theater, I usually watch an action or science fiction movie with my friends or family.
하지만, 영화관에 가면, 보통 친구나 가족과 함께 액션이나 공상과학 영화를 본다.
③
Those are the kinds of movies they enjoy.
그것들은 그들이 즐기는 종류의 영화이다.
④
Going to the theater alone was something I had never done before.
혼자 영화관에 가는 것은 내가 전에 한 번도 해 본 적이 없는 일이었다.
⑤
So, for the first challenge, I decided to give it a try.
그래서, 첫 번째 도전으로, 나는 그것을 한번 해 보기로 결심했다.
⑥
It was nice to choose the movie and the seat on my own.
영화와 좌석을 스스로 선택하는 것은 좋았다.
⑦
I didn't have to worry about anyone else's preferences.
다른 사람의 취향을 걱정할 필요가 없었다.
⑧
I saw the experience as a good opportunity to watch something different, so I chose a historical drama that I had been curious about.
나는 그 경험을 다른 것을 볼 좋은 기회로 보았고, 그래서 궁금했던 사극을 선택했다.
⑨
At first, it felt a bit awkward and lonely to sit in the theater by myself.
처음에는, 혼자 영화관에 앉아 있는 것이 조금 어색하고 외로웠다.
⑩
But once the movie started, I became completely absorbed in the story.
하지만 영화가 시작되자, 나는 이야기에 완전히 빠져들었다.
⑪
It was so satisfying just to focus on the movie itself.
영화 자체에만 집중하는 것은 매우 만족스러웠다.
⑫
There was nothing to distract me!
나를 방해하는 것이 아무것도 없었다!
⑬
This experience made me realize that I sometimes enjoy doing things alone.
이 경험은 내가 때때로 혼자 일을 하는 것을 즐긴다는 것을 깨닫게 했다.
⑭
After the movie, I promised myself that I would try to make my own choices more often and enjoy more activities by myself in the future.
영화가 끝난 후, 나는 앞으로 더 자주 스스로 선택하고 혼자 더 많은 활동을 즐기겠다고 다짐했다.
