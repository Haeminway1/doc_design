Challenge 1 — 구문 분석
① tend to V 빈도 표현
These days, I tend to watch movies on streaming services.
tend to V — '~하는 경향이 있다, ~하는 편이다'. streaming services: 스트리밍 서비스 (현재 일반적 습관)
② 조건 부사절 빈도부사
if I go to the theater, I usually watch an action or science fiction movie with my friends or family.
if절 조건문 — 현재시제로 일반적 조건을 표현. usually: 빈도부사(보통 조동사 뒤, be동사 뒤, 일반동사 앞에 위치)
③ 관계대명사 생략
Those are the kinds of movies (that) they enjoy.
관계대명사 that 생략 — 목적격 관계대명사는 생략 가능. the kinds of movies (that) they enjoy: 선행사 movies를 수식하는 관계사절
④ 동명사 주어 과거완료(경험)
Going to the theater alone was something I had never done before.
동명사 주어 — Going to the theater alone이 문장의 주어(단수 취급). had never done: 과거완료(경험) — 과거 시점 이전까지의 경험 부재를 나타내는 대과거
⑤ decide to V 숙어
So, for the first challenge, I decided to give it a try.
decide to V — '~하기로 결심하다'. give it a try: '한번 해보다' (관용 표현)
⑥ 가주어-진주어 on my own
It was nice to choose the movie and the seat on my own.
가주어 It — 진주어 to choose: 진짜 주어인 to부정사구를 문장 뒤로 보내고 형식상 It을 주어 자리에 사용. on my own: '스스로, 혼자서'
⑦ didn't have to 소유격
I didn't have to worry about anyone else's preferences.
didn't have to: '~할 필요가 없었다' (과거의 불필요). anyone else's: 소유격 — else가 부정대명사 anyone 뒤에 위치. preferences: 취향, 선호
⑧ see A as B to부정사 형용사적 용법 과거완료
I saw the experience as a good opportunity to watch something different, so I chose a historical drama that I had been curious about.
see A as B: 'A를 B로 보다[여기다]'. opportunity to watch: to부정사 형용사적 용법(opportunity 수식). that I had been curious about: 관계대명사절 + 과거완료 — 선택 시점보다 더 이전부터 궁금해했음을 표현
⑨ 가주어-진주어 by myself
it felt a bit awkward and lonely to sit in the theater by myself.
가주어 it — 진주어 to sit: felt의 보어는 awkward and lonely. by myself: '혼자서' (재귀대명사 관용 표현)
⑩ once 부사절 become absorbed in
But once the movie started, I became completely absorbed in the story.
once S+V: '일단 ~하자(마자), ~하면'. become absorbed in: '~에 빠져들다, ~에 몰입하다' (수동태 형용사 구문)
⑪ 가주어-진주어 just to V
It was so satisfying just to focus on the movie itself.
가주어 It — 진주어 to focus: satisfying은 보어. just to focus: '오직 집중하는 것만으로' — just가 to부정사를 수식하여 의미를 강조
⑫ nothing + to부정사
There was nothing to distract me!
nothing to distract: nothing을 수식하는 to부정사의 형용사적 용법 — '나를 방해할 것이 아무것도 없었다'. to distract의 의미상 목적어는 me
⑬ 사역동사 make enjoy V-ing
This experience made me realize that I sometimes enjoy doing things alone.
make + O + V원형: 사역동사 make의 목적격보어는 동사원형. that절: realize의 목적어인 명사절. enjoy V-ing: enjoy는 동명사를 목적어로 취하는 동사
⑭ 재귀대명사 would + V 비교급 부사
After the movie, I promised myself that I would try to make my own choices more often and enjoy more activities by myself in the future.
promised myself that ~: 재귀대명사 myself — 주어와 목적어가 동일한 대상. would try to V: 미래의지(과거 시제 내 미래 표현). more often: often의 비교급 부사
