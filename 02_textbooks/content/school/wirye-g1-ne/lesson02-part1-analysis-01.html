Part 1 — 구문독해
be going to V 과거분사 후치수식
①On today's show, we are going to learn about special park benches in Zimbabwe called Friendship Benches.
be going to V: 미래의 계획이나 예정을 나타내는 표현. called Friendship Benches: 과거분사구가 앞의 명사 benches를 뒤에서 수식. "우정 벤치라고 불리는"
단문
②We have two special guests.
주어(We) + 동사(have) + 목적어(two special guests)의 기본 3형식 문장.
명령문 the founder of
③First, please welcome the founder of the Friendship Bench program, Dr. Dixon Chibanda.
please + 동사원형: 공손한 명령문. the founder of: "~의 창설자". Dr. Dixon Chibanda는 the founder와 동격.
간접의문문 inspire + O + to V come up with
④Can you tell us what inspired you to come up with the program?
tell + us + 간접의문문(what inspired you): 의문사절이 목적어 역할. 어순은 의문사 + 주어 + 동사. inspire + O + to V: "~가 …하도록 영감을 주다". come up with: "생각해 내다" (숙어).
단문
⑤Sure.
단독 응답 표현. 생략된 구조: (That sounds) Sure. / Sure(, I can).
came from 관계대명사 생략 as a + 직업
⑥The idea came from an experience (that) I had as a psychiatrist.
came from: "~에서 비롯되다". an experience (that) I had: 목적격 관계대명사 that 생략. as a psychiatrist: "정신과 의사로서" — 자격·역할을 나타내는 전치사 as.
One of + 복수명사 과거완료 (변화)
⑦One of my patients had become severely depressed.
One of + 복수명사: "~중 한 명" — 주어는 단수 취급. had become: 과거완료로 과거 특정 시점 이전에 완료된 변화를 나타냄. severely: 부사, depressed 수식.
이유 부사절 (because) be unable to V afford to V
⑧However, she couldn't get help because she was unable to afford the bus ride to the hospital.
because S + V: 이유를 나타내는 종속 부사절. be unable to V: "~할 수 없다" (= cannot). afford to V: "~할 (금전적) 여유가 되다".
감정동사 (shock) 이유접속사 (as) 과거완료 (경험)
⑨This shocked me, as I had never considered such a problem before.
shock: 감정을 유발하는 타동사 — 주어가 충격을 준다. as S + V: "~이므로, ~이기 때문에" (이유 접속사). had never considered: 과거완료로 그 이전까지의 경험 없음을 강조.
In fact 가주어-진주어 의미상 주어 (for + 명사)
⑩In fact, it is difficult for many people in Zimbabwe to pay for transportation and receive medical treatment.
In fact: 담화표지 "사실은". It(가주어) ~ to V(진주어): to부정사구가 진짜 주어. for many people: to부정사의 의미상 주어. to pay와 (to) receive는 병렬 구조.
Furthermore 현재완료 there 구문 a lack of
⑪Furthermore, there has been a lack of psychiatrists in the country for many years.
Furthermore: 담화표지 "게다가" (추가 정보 연결). there has been: 현재완료 there is 구문 — 과거부터 현재까지 지속. a lack of: "~의 부족".
시간 부사절 (when) there were only 현재분사 수식
⑫When the Friendship Bench program started in 2006, there were only eleven psychiatrists serving Zimbabwe's population of over twelve million people.
When S + V(과거): 시간 부사절 "~했을 때". there were only: "단지 ~만 있었다". serving Zimbabwe's population: 현재분사구가 앞의 명사 psychiatrists를 수식.
need to V a way to V (형용사적)
⑬I needed to find a way to bring psychiatry into the community.
need + to V: "~할 필요가 있다". a way to bring: to부정사의 형용사적 용법 — "가져올 방법". bring A into B: "A를 B 안으로 가져오다".
단문
⑭Oh, so that was the beginning of it.
주어(that) + be동사(was) + 보어(the beginning of it). so: "그러니까, 그렇다면" — 이해나 확인을 나타내는 담화표지.
공손한 요청 (Could you) 간접의문문
⑮Could you explain to us how the Friendship Bench program works?
Could you + 동사원형: 정중한 요청 표현. explain to us + how S + V: 간접의문문이 explain의 목적어. 어순은 의문사 + 주어 + 동사(works).
단문
⑯Certainly.
단독 응답 표현. Sure보다 격식적인 긍정 응답.
수동태 (be created as) a way to V (형용사적)
⑰The program was created as a way to provide accessible mental health services.
was created: be + 과거분사의 수동태. as a way to provide: "제공하는 방법으로" — as는 자격·역할 전치사, to provide는 형용사적 to부정사. accessible: "접근 가능한".
offer A to B 현재분사구 수식 such as
⑱It offers talk therapy sessions to people dealing with issues such as depression or anxiety.
offer A to B: "B에게 A를 제공하다". dealing with issues: 현재분사구가 앞의 명사 people을 수식 — "문제를 겪고 있는". such as: "예를 들어, ~과 같은".
수동태 (be provided by) 분사형용사 수식
⑲The sessions are free and are provided by respected elderly women from the community.
are provided by: 수동태 — 행위자는 by 뒤에. respected: 과거분사가 형용사로 쓰여 "존경받는". respected + elderly + women: 분사 + 형용사 + 명사 순서로 수식.
과거분사 삽입절 volunteer to V 3개 동사 병렬
⑳These women, called "grandmothers," volunteer to sit with patients at the benches, listen to their stories, and provide them with emotional support.
called "grandmothers": 과거분사구가 주어 These women을 삽입절로 수식. volunteer to V: "자원하여 ~하다". sit, listen, and provide: to부정사에 연결된 3개 동사원형의 병렬 구조.
