Part 1 — 핵심 표현 · 어휘
핵심 표현
표현 의미 예문
learn about ~에 대해 배우다 We are going to learn about special park benches in Zimbabwe.
come up with (아이디어를) 생각해 내다 Can you tell us what inspired you to come up with the program?
be unable to V ~할 수 없다 She was unable to afford the bus ride to the hospital.
afford to V ~할 (금전적) 여유가 있다 She couldn't afford to pay for transportation.
it is difficult for ~ to V ~가 …하는 것이 어렵다 It is difficult for many people in Zimbabwe to pay for transportation.
a lack of ~의 부족 There has been a lack of psychiatrists in the country.
a way to V ~할 방법 I needed to find a way to bring psychiatry into the community.
deal with ~을 다루다, ~을 겪다 It offers therapy to people dealing with issues such as depression.
such as ~과 같은, 예를 들어 Issues such as depression or anxiety.
provide A with B A에게 B를 제공하다 They provide patients with emotional support.
