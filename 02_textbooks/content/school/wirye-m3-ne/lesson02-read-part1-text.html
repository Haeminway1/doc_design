Beneficial Foods for Our Bodies — Part 1
본문

①We all know that a diet containing a variety of foods keeps our bodies healthy. ②But sometimes we are not sure which foods are good for which body parts. ③Nature, however, gives us a big clue. ④Look at the following examples. ⑤Each of these foods not only looks like a certain body part but is also good for that body part.

Tomato → Heart

⑥Slice open a tomato and compare it with the human heart. ⑦You will see that they look similar. ⑧They both have multiple hollow spaces and are red. ⑨Researchers say that the chemicals that make tomatoes red are good for your heart and blood. ⑩In addition, eating tomatoes can lower your risk of heart disease.

Walnut → Brain

⑪Look at the shape of a walnut. ⑫Do you notice anything? ⑬Yes, it's very similar to the shape of the human brain! ⑭A walnut is divided into two parts, just like the brain. ⑮Walnuts also have wrinkles, which the brain has too. ⑯Studies show that walnuts help our brains stay healthy and active. ⑰They are also good for preventing Alzheimer's disease.

문장별 해석
①
We all know that a diet containing a variety of foods keeps our bodies healthy.
우리 모두는 다양한 음식을 포함하는 식단이 우리의 몸을 건강하게 유지시킨다는 것을 안다.
②
But sometimes we are not sure which foods are good for which body parts.
하지만 때때로 우리는 어떤 음식이 어떤 신체 부위에 좋은지 확신하지 못한다.
③
Nature, however, gives us a big clue.
하지만, 자연은 우리에게 큰 단서를 준다.
⑤
Each of these foods not only looks like a certain body part but is also good for that body part.
이 음식들 각각은 특정 신체 부위처럼 보일 뿐만 아니라 그 신체 부위에도 좋다.
⑥
Slice open a tomato and compare it with the human heart.
토마토를 잘라서 열어 인간의 심장과 비교해 보아라.
⑧
They both have multiple hollow spaces and are red.
그것들은 둘 다 여러 개의 빈 공간을 가지고 있고 빨간색이다.
⑨
Researchers say that the chemicals that make tomatoes red are good for your heart and blood.
연구자들은 토마토를 빨갛게 만드는 화학 물질이 심장과 혈액에 좋다고 말한다.
⑩
In addition, eating tomatoes can lower your risk of heart disease.
게다가, 토마토를 먹는 것은 심장병의 위험을 낮출 수 있다.
⑬
Yes, it's very similar to the shape of the human brain!
그렇다, 그것은 인간의 뇌의 모양과 매우 비슷하다!
⑭
A walnut is divided into two parts, just like the brain.
호두는 뇌처럼 두 부분으로 나뉜다.
⑮
Walnuts also have wrinkles, which the brain has too.
호두에도 주름이 있는데, 뇌에도 (그 주름이) 있다.
⑯
Studies show that walnuts help our brains stay healthy and active.
연구들은 호두가 우리의 뇌가 건강하고 활동적인 상태를 유지하도록 돕는다는 것을 보여준다.
⑰
They are also good for preventing Alzheimer's disease.
그것들은 또한 알츠하이머병을 예방하는 데 좋다.
