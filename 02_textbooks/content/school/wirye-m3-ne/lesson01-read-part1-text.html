본문

Why Do People Learn Foreign Languages?

①Many students learn new languages because of school requirements. ②Many others learn them for fun. ③In any case, students everywhere have found interesting ways to study new languages. ④Let's meet these students and listen to their ideas.

I Love Soccer!

⑤I'm a big fan of a Spanish soccer team. ⑥I want to understand interviews with my favorite players. ⑦However, it's not easy because I don't know Spanish that well. ⑧How can I improve my Spanish? — Owen, 16

⑨The best way to learn a new language is to practice it every day. ⑩I have changed the language of my phone to Spanish, and I have been writing my shopping lists in Spanish! — Julie, 15

⑪Some words are used only in soccer, not in everyday life. ⑫Learn some soccer vocabulary and memorize it. ⑬Also, why don't you try writing a review of a match in Spanish? ⑭It will help you improve your writing skills. — Rohan, 16

⑮What's most important is to become familiar with the language first. ⑯I suggest watching Spanish movies often. ⑰It will help you get used to the sound of the language. ⑱If the people talk too fast, try watching Spanish children's movies first. — Inho, 14

문장별 해석
①
Many students learn new languages because of school requirements.
많은 학생들이 학교 필수 조건 때문에 새로운 언어를 배운다.
②
Many others learn them for fun.
많은 다른 학생들은 재미로 그것들을 배운다.
③
In any case, students everywhere have found interesting ways to study new languages.
어쨌든, 어디에서나 학생들은 새로운 언어를 공부하는 흥미로운 방법을 찾아왔다.
④
Let's meet these students and listen to their ideas.
이 학생들을 만나 그들의 아이디어를 들어보자.
⑤
I'm a big fan of a Spanish soccer team.
나는 스페인 축구팀의 열렬한 팬이다.
⑥
I want to understand interviews with my favorite players.
나는 내가 가장 좋아하는 선수들과의 인터뷰를 이해하고 싶다.
⑦
However, it's not easy because I don't know Spanish that well.
하지만, 나는 스페인어를 그렇게 잘 알지 못하기 때문에 쉽지 않다.
⑧
How can I improve my Spanish?
어떻게 하면 내 스페인어를 향상시킬 수 있을까?
⑨
The best way to learn a new language is to practice it every day.
새로운 언어를 배우는 가장 좋은 방법은 매일 그것을 연습하는 것이다.
⑩
I have changed the language of my phone to Spanish, and I have been writing my shopping lists in Spanish!
나는 내 핸드폰의 언어를 스페인어로 바꿨고, 쇼핑 목록을 스페인어로 계속 써 오고 있다!
⑪
Some words are used only in soccer, not in everyday life.
일부 단어는 일상생활이 아닌 축구에서만 사용된다.
⑫
Learn some soccer vocabulary and memorize it.
축구 어휘를 좀 배우고 그것을 암기해라.
⑬
Also, why don't you try writing a review of a match in Spanish?
또한, 스페인어로 경기 리뷰를 써 보는 것은 어떨까?
⑭
It will help you improve your writing skills.
그것은 네 작문 실력을 향상시키는 데 도움이 될 것이다.
⑮
What's most important is to become familiar with the language first.
가장 중요한 것은 먼저 그 언어에 익숙해지는 것이다.
⑯
I suggest watching Spanish movies often.
나는 스페인 영화를 자주 보는 것을 제안한다.
⑰
It will help you get used to the sound of the language.
그것은 네가 그 언어의 소리에 익숙해지는 데 도움이 될 것이다.
⑱
If the people talk too fast, try watching Spanish children's movies first.
사람들이 너무 빨리 말하면, 먼저 스페인 어린이 영화를 시청해 보아라.
