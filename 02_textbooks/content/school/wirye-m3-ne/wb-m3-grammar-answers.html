H. 오류 찾아 고치기 (121-145)
121.have been knowing → have known (know는 상태동사로 진행형 불가)
122.The thing what → The thing that[which] (선행사가 있으면 what 불가)
123., that → , which (계속적 용법에 that 사용 불가)
124.which containing → containing (관계대명사+분사 중복, 또는 which contains)
125.suggested to watch → suggested watching (suggest + V-ing)
126.since three years → for three years (기간 앞에는 for)
127.the words what → the words that[which] (선행사가 있으면 what 불가)
128.day which → day, which (계속적 용법에 콤마 필요)
129.interesting → interested (사람이 주어이므로 과거분사 -ed)
130.are → is (동명사 주어는 단수 취급)
131.has been belonging → has belonged (belong은 상태동사로 진행형 불가)
132.The food what → The food that[which] (선행사가 있으면 what 불가)
133., that → , which (계속적 용법에 that 사용 불가)
134.played → playing (능동 의미의 현재분사로 수식)
135.from go → from going (prevent ~ from + V-ing)
136.have → has (주어 He에는 has)
137.that you need → what you need (선행사 없이 '~하는 것'은 what)
138., that → , which (계속적 용법에 that 사용 불가)
139.amazed → amazing (사물이 주어이므로 현재분사 -ing)
140.to water → water (make + 목적어 + 동사원형)
141.has been liking → has liked (like는 상태동사로 진행형 불가)
142.The language what → The language that[which] (선행사가 있으면 what 불가)
143.help you improving → help you improve (help + 목적어 + 동사원형)
144.confused → confusing (사물이 주어이므로 현재분사 -ing)
145.recommended to eat → recommended eating (recommend + V-ing)
I. 오류 3개 찾아 고치기 (146-155)
146.① have been knowing → have known (상태동사) ② the thing what → the thing that (선행사+what 불가) ③ are → is (동명사 주어 단수)
147.① , that → , which (계속적 용법) ② suggested to eat → suggested eating (suggest+V-ing) ③ surprised → surprising (사물 주어 → -ing)
148.① since two hours → for two hours (기간 → for) ② makes him to feel → makes him feel (make+O+V원형) ③ from give up → from giving up (from+V-ing)
149.① what → that[which] (선행사 nutrients가 있으므로) ② which containing → containing (관계대명사+분사 중복) ③ , that → , which (계속적 용법)
150.① has been believing → has believed (상태동사) ② help her improving → help her improve (help+O+V원형) ③ are → is (동명사 주어 단수)
151.① , that → , which (계속적 용법) ② have been eating → has been eating (주어 he → has) ③ recommended to include → recommended including (recommend+V-ing)
152.① That → What (선행사 없이 '~하는 것' → what) ② makes us to feel → makes us feel (make+O+V원형) ③ boring → bored (사람이 주어 → -ed)
153.① since three months → for three months (기간 → for) ② from speak → from speaking (from+V-ing) ③ , that → , which (계속적 용법)
154.① The method what → The method that[which] (선행사+what 불가) ② suggested to review → suggested reviewing (suggest+V-ing) ③ has been wanting → has wanted (상태동사)
155.① which containing → containing (관계대명사+분사 중복) ② help → helps (동명사 주어 단수 → 단수동사) ③ are → is (동명사 주어 단수)
