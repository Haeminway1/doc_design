M. 어순 배열 서술형 (171-185)
171.We all know that a diet containing a variety of foods keeps our bodies healthy.
172.But sometimes we are not sure which foods are good for which body parts.
173.Each of these foods not only looks like a certain body part but is also good for that body part.
174.Researchers say that the chemicals that make tomatoes red are good for your heart and blood.
175.In addition, eating tomatoes can lower your risk of heart disease.
176.A walnut is divided into two parts, just like the brain.
177.Studies show that walnuts help our brains stay healthy and active.
178.Carrots have some chemicals that can make vitamin A, which improves your vision.
179.It helps your eyes process light and send a clear image to the brain.
180.Cutting onions is not fun because it makes you cry.
181.Scientists say that onions contain vitamin B, which helps make new, healthy cells.
182.These come from a special chemical that prevents you from feeling sick and throwing up.
183.For this reason, ginger can be good for your stomach.
184.Isn't it amazing that some foods mirror the body parts that they are good for?
185.Find as many as you can and try to eat a variety of them.
