관계대명사 what ★★★
문법 개념

what = the thing(s) which / the thing(s) that

선행사를 포함하는 관계대명사 — 앞에 명사(선행사)가 올 수 없습니다!

명사절을 이끌어 문장에서 주어, 목적어, 보어 역할을 합니다.

교과서 핵심 예문
Find what keeps you motivated.
너를 동기부여하는 것을 찾아라.
what절 = 목적어 Find의 목적어 역할. what = 주어 (keeps의 주어)
keep O C keep you motivated = 너를 동기부여된 상태로 유지하다
what이 이끄는 명사절의 역할
역할예문분석
주어What I want is a new guitar.[What I want] = 주어
목적어I don't know what he said.[what he said] = know의 목적어
보어This is what I need.[what I need] = 보어
what vs that / which 구별 ★★
what (선행사 포함 ✓)that / which (선행사 필요 ✓)
✓ What I need is time. ✓ The thing that I need is time.
✗ The thing what I need... (선행사 + what 불가!) ✗ That I need is time. (선행사 없이 that 불가)
문장 전환 패턴
This is the thing which I want.
↓ the thing which → what
This is what I want.
The thing that I said was true.
↓ the thing that → what
What I said was true.
학교 프린트 출제 예상 유형 ★★★

1. 빈칸 완성: Find ______ keeps you motivated. → what

2. 어법 판단: The thing what I need is love. → ✗ (what 앞에 선행사 불가 → The thing that)

3. 밑줄 의미: what = the thing(s) which / that

4. 문장 전환: The thing which I said was true. → What I said was true.
