Inefficient teachers overlook the potential power of the opening minutes of class. Often, more than ten minutes can disappear before class starts. The first minutes set the tone for the rest of the class. If you are prepared for class and have taught your students an opening routine, they can use this brief time to make mental and emotional transitions and prepare to focus on learning new material. In summary, you should establish an opening routine to develop your class with an effective start.

16 Unit 5 · 01

The act of gardening itself is a fantastic form of physical activity. It involves a range of motions, from digging and planting to watering and harvesting. These activities help improve strength, flexibility, and endurance. Gardening is particularly beneficial for those who find traditional exercise challenging. Besides physical health, gardening has profound mental health benefits. Tending to plants can be incredibly calming and meditative, reducing stress and anxiety. Studies have shown that spending time in nature can elevate mood, improve cognition, and reduce depression symptoms.
