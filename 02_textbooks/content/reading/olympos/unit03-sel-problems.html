Unit 3 주장 / 요지
01

Inefficient teachers overlook the potential power of the opening minutes of class. Often, if students are quiet enough and if there are many pressing demands on a teacher's time at that moment, more than ten minutes can disappear before class starts. It's no wonder that students are late for class; they have little reason to be on time. You can use the first ten minutes to get your class off to a great start, or you can choose to waste this time. The first minutes set the tone for the rest of the class. If you are prepared for class and have taught your students an opening routine, they can use this brief time to make mental and emotional transitions from the last class or subject and prepare to focus on learning new material. In summary, you should establish an opening routine to develop your class with an effective start.

Q. 이 글의 요지로 가장 적절한 것은?

① 수업 시작 루틴을 정해 초반 시간을 효과적으로 활용해야 한다.

② 학생들의 지각을 방지하기 위해 벌칙 제도를 도입해야 한다.

③ 교사는 수업 준비보다 학생과의 소통에 더 집중해야 한다.

④ 수업 시간을 줄이고 자습 시간을 늘려야 한다.

⑤ 학생들의 집중력을 높이기 위해 시청각 자료를 활용해야 한다.

04

As the parent of a gifted child, you need to be aware of a certain common parent trap. Of course you are a proud parent, and you should be. While it is very easy to talk nonstop about your little genius and his or her remarkable behavior, this can be very stressful on your child. It is extremely important to limit your bragging behavior to your very close friends, or your parents. Gifted children feel pressured when their parents show them off too much. This behavior creates expectations that they may not be able to live up to, and also creates a false sense of self for your child. You want your child to be who they are, not who they seem to be as defined by their incredible achievements. If not, you could end up with a driven perfectionist child or perhaps a drop-out, or worse.

Q. 이 글의 요지로 가장 적절한 것은?

① 영재 자녀의 성취를 지나치게 자랑하면 아이에게 부정적 영향을 줄 수 있다.

② 영재 교육 프로그램에 일찍 등록할수록 효과적이다.

③ 부모는 자녀의 재능을 적극적으로 홍보해야 한다.

④ 완벽주의 성향은 학업 성취에 긍정적인 영향을 준다.

⑤ 자녀의 자존감은 부모의 칭찬량에 비례하여 형성된다.
