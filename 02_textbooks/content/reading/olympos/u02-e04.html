Unit 2 · 04 — 문장별 해석
문장별 해석
①
All the actors on the stage were focused on their acting.
무대 위의 모든 배우들은 연기에 집중하고 있었다.
②
Then, suddenly, Arthur fell into the corner of the stage.
그때 갑자기 Arthur가 무대 구석으로 쓰러졌다.
③
Jeevan immediately approached Arthur and found his heart wasn't beating.
Jeevan은 즉시 Arthur에게 다가가 그의 심장이 뛰지 않는 것을 발견했다.
④
Jeevan began CPR.
Jeevan은 심폐소생술을 시작했다.
⑤
Jeevan worked silently, glancing sometimes at Arthur's face.
Jeevan은 조용히 작업하면서 때때로 Arthur의 얼굴을 흘끗 보았다.
⑥
He thought, "Please, start breathing again, please."
그는 생각했다, "제발, 다시 숨 쉬어 줘, 제발."
⑦
Arthur's eyes were closed.
Arthur의 눈은 감겨 있었다.
⑧
Moments later, an older man in a grey suit appeared, swiftly kneeling beside Arthur's chest.
잠시 후, 회색 양복을 입은 나이 든 남자가 나타나 Arthur의 가슴 옆에 재빠르게 무릎을 꿇었다.
⑨
"I'm Walter Jacobi.
"저는 Walter Jacobi입니다.
⑩
I'm a doctor." He announced with a calm voice.
의사입니다." 그가 차분한 목소리로 말했다.
⑪
Jeevan wiped the sweat off his forehead.
Jeevan은 이마의 땀을 닦았다.
⑫
With combined efforts, Jeevan and Dr. Jacobi successfully revived Arthur.
합동 노력으로 Jeevan과 Jacobi 박사는 성공적으로 Arthur를 소생시켰다.
⑬
Arthur's eyes slowly opened.
Arthur의 눈이 천천히 떠졌다.
⑭
Finally, Jeevan was able to hear Arthur's breath again, thinking to himself, "Thank goodness. You're back."
마침내 Jeevan은 Arthur의 숨소리를 다시 들을 수 있었고, 속으로 생각했다, "다행이다. 돌아왔어."
Unit 2 · 04 — 구문 분석
① be focused on
All the actors on the stage were focused on their acting.
be focused on: ~에 집중하다
③ find (that) S+V
Jeevan immediately approached Arthur and found his heart wasn't beating.
find (that) S + V: ~임을 알다/발견하다
⑤ 분사구문 (동시동작)
Jeevan worked silently, glancing sometimes at Arthur's face.
glancing: 현재분사 — 동시동작 (분사구문)
⑧ 분사구문 (연속동작)
Moments later, an older man in a grey suit appeared, swiftly kneeling beside Arthur's chest.
swiftly kneeling: 현재분사 — 동시동작/연속동작 (분사구문)
⑪ wipe A off B
Jeevan wiped the sweat off his forehead.
wipe A off B: B에서 A를 닦아내다
⑫ With + N (수단)
With combined efforts, Jeevan and Dr. Jacobi successfully revived Arthur.
With + N: ~으로/~을 가지고 (수단). combined: 과거분사 (합동의)
⑭ be able to V 분사구문 (동시동작)
Finally, Jeevan was able to hear Arthur's breath again, thinking to himself, "Thank goodness. You're back."
be able to V: ~할 수 있다
thinking: 분사구문 (동시동작)
