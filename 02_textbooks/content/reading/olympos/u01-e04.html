Unit 1 · 04 — 문장별 해석
문장별 해석
①
To whom it may concern,
관계자분께,
②
I am writing to express my deep concern about the recent change made by Pittsburgh Train Station.
Pittsburgh 기차역의 최근 변경 사항에 대한 깊은 우려를 표명하고자 편지를 드립니다.
③
The station had traditional ticket offices with staff before, but these have been replaced with ticket vending machines.
이전에 역에는 직원이 있는 전통적인 매표소가 있었지만, 이것이 무인 발매기로 대체되었습니다.
④
However, individuals who are unfamiliar with these machines are now experiencing difficulty accessing the railway services.
그러나 이 기계에 익숙하지 않은 사람들은 현재 철도 서비스 이용에 어려움을 겪고 있습니다.
⑤
Since these individuals heavily relied on the staff assistance to be able to travel, they are in great need of ticket offices with staff in the station.
이 사람들은 여행할 수 있도록 직원의 도움에 크게 의존했기 때문에, 역에 직원이 있는 매표소가 절실히 필요합니다.
⑥
Therefore, I am urging you to consider reopening the ticket offices.
따라서 매표소 재개를 고려해 주실 것을 촉구합니다.
⑦
With the staff back in their positions, many people would regain access to the railway services.
직원이 다시 자리에 복귀하면, 많은 사람들이 철도 서비스에 대한 접근성을 회복할 것입니다.
⑧
I look forward to your prompt attention to this matter and a positive resolution.
이 문제에 대한 신속한 관심과 긍정적인 해결을 기대합니다.
⑨
Sincerely, Sarah Roberts
Sarah Roberts 올림
Unit 1 · 04 — 구문 분석
② to부정사 (목적) 과거분사 (후치수식)
I am writing to express my deep concern about the recent change made by Pittsburgh Train Station.
to express: ~하기 위해 (목적의 to부정사)
made by ~: ~에 의해 만들어진 (과거분사, change 수식)
③ 현재완료 수동태 replace A with B
The station had traditional ticket offices with staff before, but these have been replaced with ticket vending machines.
have been replaced: 현재완료 수동태
replace A with B: A를 B로 대체하다
④ 관계대명사 who experience difficulty V-ing
However, individuals who are unfamiliar with these machines are now experiencing difficulty accessing the railway services.
who are unfamiliar with: 관계대명사절 (individuals 수식)
experience difficulty V-ing: ~하는 데 어려움을 겪다. be unfamiliar with: ~에 익숙하지 않다
⑤ Since (이유) be in need of
Since these individuals heavily relied on the staff assistance to be able to travel, they are in great need of ticket offices with staff.
Since: ~이기 때문에 (이유의 접속사). rely on: ~에 의존하다
to be able to V: ~할 수 있도록. be in need of: ~이 필요하다
⑥ urge + O + to V consider V-ing
I am urging you to consider reopening the ticket offices.
urge + O + to V: O에게 V하도록 촉구하다
consider V-ing: ~하는 것을 고려하다
⑦ With + O + 부사구 (부대상황)
With the staff back in their positions, many people would regain access to the railway services.
With + O + 부사구: 부대상황 (~한 상태로/~하면)
would: 가정적 미래
⑧ look forward to + N
I look forward to your prompt attention to this matter and a positive resolution.
look forward to + N: ~을 기대하다 (to는 전치사)
