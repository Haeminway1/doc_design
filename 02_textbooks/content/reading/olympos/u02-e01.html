Unit 2 · 01 — 문장별 해석
문장별 해석
①
I glanced at the clock on the wall.
벽에 걸린 시계를 흘끗 보았다.
②
10:00.
10시.
③
That meant the casting director would call very soon with the results of my first audition for a musical part in The Wizard of Oz.
그것은 캐스팅 디렉터가 《오즈의 마법사》 뮤지컬 파트 첫 오디션 결과를 곧 전화로 알려줄 것이라는 뜻이었다.
④
I felt shaky all over, chewing my thumbnail and jiggling my feet.
나는 온몸이 떨렸고, 엄지손톱을 물어뜯으며 발을 들썩이고 있었다.
⑤
Finally, the telephone rang.
마침내 전화벨이 울렸다.
⑥
While I was coming round, Dad answered.
내가 정신을 차리는 동안 아빠가 전화를 받았다.
⑦
I heard him say, "Ahh, thank you. I'll let her know ..."
"아, 감사합니다. 딸에게 알려줄게요..."라고 말하는 것이 들렸다.
⑧
As I got to the bottom of the stairs, he was just putting the phone down.
내가 계단 아래에 도착했을 때, 아빠는 막 수화기를 내려놓고 있었다.
⑨
"That was The Wizard of Oz. You're second senior munchkin," he announced.
"오즈의 마법사에서 전화 왔어. 네가 두 번째 시니어 먼치킨이래," 아빠가 말했다.
⑩
I got a little rush of excitement, knowing I was in — that whatever happened I could be involved in one of the productions.
나는 짧은 흥분의 감정이 밀려왔는데, 내가 합격했다는 것 — 무슨 일이 있더라도 그 공연에 참여할 수 있다는 것을 알았기 때문이다.
Unit 2 · 01 — 구문 분석
① glance at
I glanced at the clock on the wall.
glance at: ~을 흘끗 보다
③ mean (that) S+V would (시제 일치)
That meant the casting director would call very soon with the results of my first audition.
mean (that) S + V: ~을 의미하다
would: 과거 시점의 미래 (시제 일치)
④ 분사구문 (동시동작)
I felt shaky all over, chewing my thumbnail and jiggling my feet.
shaky: 떨리는 (형용사, felt의 보어)
chewing / jiggling: 현재분사 — 동시동작 (분사구문)
⑥ While S + V (시간) come round
While I was coming round, Dad answered.
While S + V: ~하는 동안 (시간의 접속사)
come round: 정신을 차리다
⑦ hear + O + V원형 (지각동사) ★ let + O + V원형 (사역동사)
I heard him say, "Ahh, thank you. I'll let her know ..."
hear + O + V원형: O가 V하는 것을 듣다 (지각동사)
let + O + V원형: O가 V하게 하다 (사역동사)
⑧ As (시간) 과거진행형
As I got to the bottom of the stairs, he was just putting the phone down.
As: ~할 때 (시간의 접속사)
was putting: 과거진행형. just: 막 ~하고 있던 (부사)
⑩ 분사구문 (이유) whatever (양보) ★
I got a little rush of excitement, knowing I was in — that whatever happened I could be involved in one of the productions.
knowing: 분사구문 (이유 — ~이기 때문에). I was in: 합격했다 (구어 표현)
whatever happened: 양보의 복합관계대명사 (= no matter what happened). be involved in: ~에 참여하다
