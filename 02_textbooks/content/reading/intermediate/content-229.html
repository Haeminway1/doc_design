The "noosphere," a concept jointly developed by philosopher Pierre Teilhard de Chardin and scientist Vladimir Vernadsky, posits the emergence of a "sphere of human thought" as the next stage of Earth's evolution. Following the geosphere (inanimate matter) and the biosphere (biological life), the noosphere represents the collective consciousness and intelligence of humanity enveloping the planet. For Teilhard, this was a teleological process, a directed evolution toward an ultimate point of spiritual unification he called the "Omega Point."
